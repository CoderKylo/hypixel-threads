Razer Viper Mini.

I bought a model o and it broke in the course of 3 months and got glossy from just holding it in about a month. I only lightly butterfly clicked. I got a replacement and the same exact thing happened on that model o. I bought a viper mini and NONE of these problems were present, it did mean giving up double-clicking but honestly double-clicking is overrated. So far, it's lasted me 5 months, it isn't glossy, it isn't broken, I still have it in perfect condition, time is still ticking. Not to mention the build quality is better and the look and shape of the mouse is better including the software and the switches just feel so much better.
