I may be late to the conversation, but I wanted to state my opinion.

It really depends. If you want to play it mainly, then go ahead. Resource packs literally change the entire UI without mods. Performance is miles ahead of Java, and, contrary to popular belief (Trust me, I used to believe this too. You need to see it to believe it), servers are actually cooler in some aspects, as they support things Java doesn't and, most importantly, Cubecraft and The Hive are the two largest servers on Bedrock, at 30k Members peak, each. Why is this important? 2 servers, no monopolies. There is no Hypixel there to literally destroy everything else and dominate at 216k while other servers are sitting at a maximum of 30k.

I also like The Hive PvP more than I do on Hypixel. Hive's main priority is Gameplay, and that really shows, as the games are really well designed.

So, if you want to play there for a nice amount of time, then I would say, yes, buy it, and play on The Hive, as most PC players play there.

Though I will say, it's not easy in the beginning.
