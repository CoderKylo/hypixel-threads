Background Information
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I joined Minecraft at 1.10, but got java at 1.14. I've been playing Hypixel for a little more than a year.
and of course like anyone else I bought a rank. I was originally thinking about MVP, but got MVP+ Instead.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VIP+ and MVP Explained
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VIP+ is a rank you can buy for 14.99 on the Hypixel Store. MVP is rank
you can buy for 29.99. Seems normal right? VIP+ has some pretty cool features.
Like a firework, or Housing Mailboxes. On the store, VIP+ has 8/27 features available.
MVP, has well, the same amount. Worst part, you're paying $10 dollars more for a blue name tag.
I know most people know about this, but its just dumb.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What you SHOULD buy instead.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
MVP+ is the best rank to buy, it even says it on the store. If you have the LITTLE extra money,
you can buy this, and it is a lifetime thing! And if you want to get MVP++, you can buy it as a temporary thing.

-----------------------------------------------------------------
Conclusion
----------------------------------------------------------------------
MVP is by far the WORST rank to buy if you don't want to waste money. Its just a blue version of VIP+. I really didn't have to make this, but I just wanna spread the message (even though most people know). Yeah soooooooooo dont buy it
