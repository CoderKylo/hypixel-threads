Wrote this in class lol

Kid does tiktok challenges, lives to regret it

Kid: wow i found this new tiktok challenge about swearing at your mother, i should try it out

Kid: hey mom, I hope both sides of your pillows are WARM TONGIHT

Mother: *gasp* how could you say that, you know what, NO MORE VIDEO GAMES TONIGHT

Kid: idc im gonna get so much likes

Kid goes back to his room, with his tiktok video set up, and posted it

14 hours later… he wakes up and checks his tiktok video, 19 likes

Kid goes downstairs, tells mother; MOOOM I GOT 19 LIKES ON TIKTOK

Mother: how do you get likes on tictac

Kid takes out phone and records mother mistaking tiktok for tictac

Kid: HAHHAA YOU SAID TIKTOK HAHAHAHAAHAHHA

Mother: what?

Kid; ha you should be in a 10 iq dream compilation

Mother: whats dream

Kid: ha ur so dumb dumb, don't even know who dream is hahahaahahha

Kid goes up to his room, and posts the video, gains 5 likes, “nice” he says.

Kid finds a new tiktok challenge called “smash all the eggs in your mother’s fridge”

Kid goes downstairs and finds one egg

Kid thinks: i should throw this at my stupid mom and get more likes!

Kid goes to find mother in the car and opens the door

Mother: what are you doing?

Kid throws egg at her face

Kid: HAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA UR SO BAD L L L L

Mother: WAHT THE HECK COME BACK HERE

Kid pushes mother and she falls on her head

Kid; hhahahahahaahhaa u suck

Mother: you know what, im sending you to discipline camp for 3 days.

Kid: so? I can make more tiktoks haahhaha

Kid goes to his room and posts the video, and gains 24 likes

Kid then makes another tiktok video stating that hes going to discipline camp

Many people warn him that is going to be rough, but he ignores as always

Mother drives him to his discipline camp

Discpliner: hello fellow camper, welcome, so mother just to let you know you can come pick him up anytime but it's going to be quite rough.

Kid: hahahhaa so what? Im so strong!

Discpliner: not in this camp

Kid: who cares im gonna make more tiktoks!
Discpliner: well were going to take away your phone

Kid: what? No im going to SUE you

Mother: take his phone away please

Kid: what? Well i can make the tiktoks after, this is going to be easy

Discpliner: oh buddy, im gonna say that it's not gonna be easy

Kid: yea right

Kid goes to his dorm

Kid: what is this? There's no pc? HOW AM I SUPPOSED TO PLAY HYPIXEL SKYBLOCK
DIscpliner: that the neat part, you don't

Kid: ugh

Kid goes to sleep and gets woken up at 5am by the discpliner

Dis: WAKE UP WAKE UP, GET READY FOR SHOVELING THE DRIVEWAY?

Kid; what? No im not going

Dis: DID YOU JUST TALK BACK TO ME? Listen kid YOUR GOING TO SHOVEL THE DRIVEWAY WHETHER YOU FLIPPIN LIKE IT OR NOT!

Kid is startled and proceeds to cry

Dis: I DON'T CARE IF YOURE CRYING OR NOT YOURE GOING TO SHOVEL THE DRIVEWAY YOU HEAR ME?

Kid finally agrees and spends 2 minutes shoveling the driveway

Dis: what are you DOING, KEEP SHOVELING

Kid: but im tiiiiired

Dis: i don't goddam care, KEEP SHOUVELING

Kid: fine!

Kid stays at the camp for 2 more days and finally gets picked up

Mother; so how was it honey

Kid: so ezz!

Mother: guess i should keep you there

Kid: noo please i lied it was so tough

Mother: guess it taught you right

Kid then learns his lesson, as to not disrespect your mother, and goes home and plays some roblox and finally sleeps in his nice comfortable bed

THE END
