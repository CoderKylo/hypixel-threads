this thread is devoted on teaching you to master the art of sh*tposting. "what is sh*tposting?" you might ask, and if you did ask that ur dumb af (hint: it's in the name).

Anyways, moving on, there are 3 things that you need to master to become a god of sh*tposting. Those things are: Time management, clickbait and humor.

To start off lets start with time management.
It is important to give yourself a small time frame, as you would be better off making an effort post than a sh*tpost if you take the same amount of time for them. For YouTube videos I would recommend giving yourself a max editing time of 0:30h-1:30h, and for forums and text-based sh*tposts I recommend 10-30 minutes.

Now, to make sure your time is well spent and gets clicks, you want a good clickbait.
For Minecraft I would recommend going into creative and making a quick screenshot of yourself holding something "unobtainable!" or "super rare!". for other games (I doubt you will be interested since this is Hypixel forums) you will want stuff considered "cool" or "top 1%". Clickbait is one of the most important parts of sh*tposting as it is the main thing that gets you views and reactions. Also remember to make a really clickbaity title.

Of course though, if you give a **** about your rep on forums, you want good reactions. that's why you need humor.
Humor is no doubt one of the hardest things to do in sh*tposting. First of all, instead of hiding the fact that your sh*tposting, embrace it! It makes good humor and makes it so less people hate on you for it. You also want to put tons of random stuff because frankly, random = funny to like 90% of internet users.

That is your guide to the 3 things that make up sh*tposting! hope you enjoyed and gl farming reactions!
