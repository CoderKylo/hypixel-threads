A lot of people in the Minecraft community won't buy a mouse if it can't double click, and here are some reasons why you shouldn't filter yourself to only mice that can double click.


1. Double clicking is considered a problem, and more and more companies are solving the double clicking issue. This really narrows down how many mice you can purchase.
2. Double clicking is literally destroying your mouse, you are just beating up those switches which mostly only have 20 million clicks, which if you actively double click you will reach.
3. Most double clicking mice are cheap or made by a company new to making gaming mice. For example, I've had two Glorious Model O mice, and both of them broke within 3 months of lightly butterfly clicking.
4. Double clicking is not essential and you can easily play the game and be good without it, normal clicking is way better than you think.
5. You can't aim with most of these insane clicking methods.
6. A lot of these mice are overly expensive for what they are (Looking at you, Bloody and Glorious.)
7. A lot of these mice are not practical at all if you switch games, you aren't going to play just Minecraft forever.
8. Some of these mice are bricks (Looking at you, Bloody)
9. Most of them are overhype and aren't as good as people say. (Looking at you, glorious.)
10. Overall mice that don't double click usually are better, mainly Logitech and Razer mice.
11. The mice that don't double click are usually higher quality, they usually don't pay attention to their mouse being very good if it has the switch problem that causes double clicking
12. (based on my experience) Razer mice have better build quality, better switches, better feel, better coating and shape than the model o.
13. If you abuse your mouse it isn't going to last nowhere near as long as one that you don't abuse.

just something to consider, you shouldn't just filter yourself to mice that double click, try something like a razer viper mini, you won't be disappointed.
