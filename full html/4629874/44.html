So, it's that time of the year again where discord and Xbox team up and bestows to us some discord nitro! I'm creating this thread for users that aren't aware of this promo or just need some extra straight-to-the-point clarification. Also, you save some money from not falling victim to scalpers. For more info on the promo from discord, click here. Enjoy!

Before I start, let me first mention that your first month of Xbox Game Pass on ultimate is $1.
Once you subscribe, you can easily cancel your subscription to avoid further charges after your first month. How do I cancel my subscription to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate? How about for discord nitro, I don't want to get charged after three months!

Xbox Game Pass Ultimate Cancellation - Click here
Discord Nitro Cancellation - Click here

Step 1: Create a Microsoft Account Online - Click here
Step 2: Subscribe to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate - Click here
Step 3: Download the Xbox app for Windows - Click here
Step 4: Open the Xbox App - Sign in and head to your perks section
Step 5: Scroll down until you see the promo "Discord Nitro"
Step 6: Click the promo and you'll be redirected to Discord to claim your nitro

Note: Please make sure you have the correct discord account signed into on your browser because it will add it to that account and not the account you have signed into on the desktop app. Also, you will need to add a billing method on file to claim your discord nitro.

If this helped you, a comment or a thumbs up will be much appreciated.
