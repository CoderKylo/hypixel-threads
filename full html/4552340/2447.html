>What is a RedBar% speedrun?

Among the speedrunning community, one of the most renowned speedruns is the Hypixel Forums RedBar% speedrun. We're currently awaiting an actual speedrun.com category (you know we just need a moderator for that right? Not sure if the forums count as a video game lol) but it's obviously still a highly popular category followed by millions of people... I think.

However, RedBar% speedruns are incredibly difficult to perform and are not for the faint-hearted. This speedrun requires actual skill, unlike all other video game speedruns and perhaps even some Dream-level luck. I have produced a list of themost common strategies for RedBar%'s below.

[>Trollposting.

One of the most widely used strats is trollposting. This involves purposefully posting ****posts or controversial content that will gain negative reactions in order to make your reaction bar fully red, hence the name RedBar%.

Advantages:

-Trollposting is easy to do and requires little effort: the less effort, the better!
-You can mix this with things like necroposting to increase your negative reaction count!
-This strat is incredibly efficient with a New Member rank.

Disadvantages:

-Trollposting purpsefully is annoying and frowned upon.
-People may catch on to intentional trolls and not give you the negative reactions you're looking for!

>Posting opinions.

Opinions. On the internet?! What were you even thinking?!! While this strat is incredibly risky, the reward is high, especially if your opinion has to do with something especially controversial. *coughcoughfreelookcough* What? No, there are no opinions here, this is a completely formal thread.

Advantages:

-High reward
-Can last for a long time and provide constant negative reactions depending on the subject.

Disadvantages:

-Making a believable and effort opinion post can take long, but it is practically indistinguishable from an actual opinion!
-People will say mean things (

Please don't do any of this stuff.

The forums has enough trolls as it is.

I hope this thread assists you in future RedBar% runs. These tactics can really improve your time! Until next time on Speedforums!
