@RevenantShadow is notoriously known for speedrunning forums and reaching 10k messages in less than half a year.

Currently, he's at 12.3k messages and joined the forums on March 6, 2021

Today is October 19. There are 227 days between March 6th and October 19th.

Each day has 24 hours. 24*227=5448 hours.

12300/5448=2.2577092511

That would mean every hour, revenant would post a total of 2.2577092511 messages.


However, school, sleep, homework and eating exist, even for a rat. Considering revenant sleeps an average of 8 hours per day, that would mean

8*227=1816

5448-1816=3632

12300/3632=3.38656387665 messages posted per hour.

However, that's not even close to the end.

Since revenant doesn't want to disclose where he lives (), all ik is that he lives in 'murica. The average school day lasts for 6.64 hours, but I'm just gonna round that to 7 to make it easier for me.

The summer break in 'murica is 88 days according to google.

227-88=139

139*7=973 hours

3632-973=2659

12300/2659=4.62579917262

I have no idea how old revenant is (15-16 ish maybe?) and how school works in 'murica, but I can probably make a safe assumption revenant spends approx 2 hours every day on homework. Also that he spends like maybe an hour every day eating in total?

139*2=278

227*1=227

227+268=495

2659-495=2164

12300/2164=5.68391866913

In conclusion, in the assumption revenant does nothing except eat sleep school homework and forums, he would be posting at least 5-6 times every hour on the forums on average.

And considering revenant plays a lot of hypixel, probably has an actual life, and has friends and a loving family, he's probably posting at least 10-11 every hour on the forums on average.

@RevenantShadow take break from forums when
