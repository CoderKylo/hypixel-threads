Facts About Withers


• The very first and most known fact on the forums, Centranos is a wither. If you look at @AlphaCloud forumer texture pack, you can see that he is in fact the wither. Also his projectiles, the wither skulls are in fact another game master @BOGA32, who is a very cool dude, alongside Centranos, I dont know what the other mods and admins are like so dont flame me.

• The wither is used as a lego building set. (@Planet would love this) Its described that you have to build the TNT cannon and wage battle against the fearsome wither and wither skeletons that protect the nether fortress. Enjoy hands-on Minecraft adventures featuring your favorite characters and objects with this LEGO Minecraft set - designed for young fans of the highly successful sandbox video game.

• Did you know that withers are the highest part of a horse's back, lying at the base of the neck above the shoulders. The height of a horse is measured to the withers.

• The word wither is in fact part of the name of an insurance firm. Their slogan is:

Since 1896 we have been trusted advisors to successful people and businesses with complex legal needs, in good times and bad.​


• Wither is also used as the name of another insurance firm located where I live in fact, in New York City

• Wither was the last name of Bill Withers, a singer whos made songs like “Aint No Sunshine” and “Lovely Day”

• Wither is not only used in the name of two insurance firms but also in a furniture store, selling custom furniture and interiors.

•In minecraft story mode, the wither has evolved to new heights. Due to the power of the command block, it turned into a wither storm. This mob is damn near unstoppable and its a scene that forces you to save one of your friends and let the other die. (In the game of course)

• Last but definitely not least, it is the name of a minecraft boss known as the wither. He can be spawned with 4 soul sand places in a t shape with 3 wither skeleton heads on the top of each block on the top of the t shape. When spawned, he has phases such as where he can not be affected by bows. He can also attack the player with two attacks, shooting wither skulls, or creating a massive explosion that kills anything near it. The wither boss also has 150 player hearts and can sometimes break obsidian. However in bedrock edition, that number is multiplied by 2, giving it 300 player hearts. Whats also interesting is how on bedrock edition, it shoots two types of wither skull projectiles. It shoots black and blue wither skulls. The black wither skulls move quickly and there black skulls can destroy blocks under blast protection 4. Blue Wither skulls move slower and can destroy all blocks equally except for bedrock and end portal frames. When either of these skulls hit players, they deal eight player hearts of damage and turn player hearts black. When killed, it drops a nether star that can be crafted into a beacon. Beacons give positive effects depending on what items it was built on and what ore was placed inside it to power it.

So the term wither…
Is the identity of a hypixel forums moderator
Is used in the names of two insurance firms
A furniture and interior store
A part of a horse
The last name of a singer known as Bill Withers
The mob that was grown like mario into the wither storm in minecraft story mode.
And of course… the wither boss in minecraft
These are the terms the word wither was used in that I could find
Have a nice day!
