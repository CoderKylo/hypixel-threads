So for my last day in Taiwan, I decided to do something special. I built Taipei 101 in Minecraft. It took a long time (7 hours) and I got help from my brother and structure blocks.
Here’s the vid:
I have been to Taipei 101 3 times I think, but only once to the observation deck (floor 89 and 91). I don’t remember going on the 101th floor though.
Some fun facts:
  • It was the tallest building in the world from 2004 to 2010
  • It is currently the 10th tallest building in the world

  • It had the fastest elevator until 2016, with a speed of 1,010 meters per minute (about 60.6 kilometers per hour). So it took 37 seconds to go from floor 5 to 89
  • It has a tuned mass damper to stop it from collapsing during earthquakes and typhoons. It’s like this giant metal ball that, you know what? Read the Wikipedia page. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taipei_101#Structural_design It’s hard to explain.
  • It had a huge Apple store (MAC GANG)
  • Final fun fact: I bet @Melody529 has been there AT LEAST ONCE in her lifetime.
I’LL MISS YOU TAIWAN!!!
