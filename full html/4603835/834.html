We have gotten 3 mobs to choose from for the mob vote during the 2021 Minecraft Live Livestream.

The first one is the Glare. The glare primary use is to let the player know when the light level is dark enough for mobs to spawn. This may seem useful, but you know can tell if mobs can spawn in the area... if mobs are spawning in that area. Seems obvious, so it might be a bit tedious to go out of your way to find a glare to tell you if it dark or not. Out of all of them, this one may have the least amount of use.

Next up is the Allay. It dances to note blocks and it likes music. If you give it an item, it will pick up more of the same item in the area, and it will drop the items next to note blocks. I can see this being useful in large farms, with the allays as an automatic collection system. It may be cheaper then using hundreds of iron and wood to make hoppers or gathering ice to make water runways. Wolves can't fetch in Minecraft, but the allay can.

Finally we have the Copper Golem. You can build it wherever you want, similar to the iron and snow golem. It will randomly push copper buttons. I don't see this as too useful, as you can create redstone clocks to make timed signals. However, I think the copper button could be very unique. Wooden buttons remain active for 15 redstone ticks, and stone buttons for 10. So maybe, the copper button will have it's own timing. Also, the copper golem has a very short life. It will turn into a statue after a specific amount of time. If there is a way to add wax to the copper golem to keep it from turning to a statue or extending it's life, that would be great.

During the mob vote, there are two round of voting, and one mob will be eliminated during the first one. Which mob do you think will be eliminated first. What mob do you think will win? Hopefully, we won't regret it this time.
