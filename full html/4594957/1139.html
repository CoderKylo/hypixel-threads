DISCLAIMER: In no way am I saying 1.8 is a bad version of Minecraft, or that hypixel should update.


So if you've been on the hypixel forums for more than 5 minutes, you've probably seen at least one suggestion saying hypixel should update to the latest version of Minecraft. You may have clicked on one of these suggestions, and if you looked at the comments, you almost undoubtedly saw that about 99.9% of them said stuff like "no" or "You idiot hypixel should never update" or "1.8 is best no other versions exist for me" or even "Mojang is ruining the game." While I personally don't think hypixel should update, no one seems to realize that these are just suggestions, not "hYpIxEl HaS tO dO tHiS oR iT's ThE wOrSt SeRvEr EvEr." Not only that, but there are real life people behind those posts. The toxicity of the 1.8 community is more often than not going to drive new players away. This means that if 1.8 players quit, there's no new people to fill in hypixel's playercount. Now, sure you could say this is all just a Theory... A GAME THEORY... and you'd be right. I don't have any data to back this up. But if you just think, what I'm saying makes sense. So I encourage 1.8 players to be like me. What does this mean? It doesn't mean rickroll people, or make lore for hypixel, or anything similar. It means try to play both the latest version and 1.8 equally; 1.8 for hypixel, and 1.17 for other servers. No, mojang isn't ruining Minecraft. Neither version is better than the other in my opinion. After all...


#MINECRAFTISMINECRAFT
