Last night, I discovered that I was able to customize my notification sound and I had the worst idea ever. Here's how it goest:

Step 1: Go on YouTube and search up "Vine Thud Sound Effect"
Step 2: Download the mp3
Step 3: Go to your files within your phone
Step 4: Put the mp3 into the Notifications folder
Step 5: Go onto Settings and tap on wherever Notification settings are
Step 6: Go to App Notifications and select the app
Step 7: Choose that sound on whatever you want to be notified about
Step 8: Turn up your notification sound to 100%
Step 9: Wait
