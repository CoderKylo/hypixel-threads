Alright so if yall don’t know I am absolutely addicted to Pizza Rolls so I figured that I’d review all of the different flavors I’ve eaten for literally no reason.


To start off we have pepperoni pizza rolls. These are an absolute classic, a good go-to choice.

We then have the triple cheese. If you don’t care for pepperoni or meat then these would be great. The cheeses together are amazing and it tastes great.

Triple meat is also a good choice, especially if you like meat, duh. These aren’t near the top of my list but they still smack every once in a while.

There is also the combination pizza rolls. I’m actually eating these right now. I don’t even know what’s in these pizza rolls but it hits different imo.

Now my absolute favorite pizza roll flavor is the Takis Fuego pizza rolls. These things literally light my mouth on fire but they are just so good, I can’t resist them. These may be my favorite tbh.


I think these are all of the different flavors I’ve had so far. If you have any other recommendations then please tell me. And if you haven't had them before pls try them you won't regret it.
