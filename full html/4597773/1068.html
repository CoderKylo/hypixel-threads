This is not a hate forum just to be clear!
Most of you clicking on this have probably heard of the hypixel youtuber SuchSpeed. I have seen his videos since late 2021, and at first I was confused. He would promote clients, and say that it was the new best. His videos would clickbait things, and that doesnt really affect hypixel, but here is where things get bad. His recent videos have been promotion things that are blatantly breaking hypixels rules. He has promoted Cracked Minecraft, Autoclicking (some mouse videos where he uses firekey), and other things that are against hypixel's rules. Here is a description from one video: "So I downloaded a FREE Minecraft Hack Client for my gaming mouse which I used to boost my clicks from 10 CPS to 50 CPS in Minecraft to find out if clicking faster makes a difference. I wouldn't recommend trying this yourself unless you want to get banned on Hypixel, should I giveaway my 2 Razer mice, let me know." SuchSpeed from "The FREE Mouse Hack Client". Sometimes he says that making the video may get his youtube rank taken away.

Disclaimer: Some videos are not breaking rules on hypixel but he is literally autoclicking and using macros, and as a content creator he should know better than to use that in videos where he has an audience of over 350k.

I may be completly wrong, and he is doing nothing breaking the rules. I do not intent to send hate, or propose hate, but I feel something needs to be done about this.
