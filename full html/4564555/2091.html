This is the first thread of a series of threads that I plan on doing with the name being "Nostalgia Series". The idea is to slowly go back through the years and show what was popular in each year. It'll start from 2021 and slowly work it's way down. The idea is to show how quickly time goes by and how our society progresses in unique ways and the many different impacts our generation has caused. If I've missed anything let me know, I've tried to pick out stuff that would mostly apply to this audience and be interesting.

-The amount of new pop stars coming onto the stage and facing off against the big players, people such as Billie Eillish, Lil Nas X and Lizzo broke into the industry with significant impacts. Some of the songs made by these people are Bad Guy, Old Town Road, and Good As Hell, but there are even more songs and creators too!

-For those that are fashion conscious you may have noticed that in 2020 the colours were dialed down to more down to earth colours, quite literally. Earthy Naturals were very prominent with less neon colours popping up showing colours were getting toned down to more natural ones such as olive green, caramel brown or rusty colours.

-For those in sports there are now 4 new sports are added for the 2020 olympic games which consist of Climbing, Karate, Skateboarding and surfing. Baseball and softball will also be returning back to the games after a break from the 2008 games. There would also be 9 mixed gender events too.

-For those of you into food but more specifically vegan/plant based food then you may know about this. Beyond meat is steamrolling gaining more popularity as days go on and making more and more deals with brands such as Burger King, KFC, Subway all to bring affordable yet vegan options to the masses.

-For those of you old enough to drink which is most likely not a lot, 0% alcohol drinks are becoming ever more popular with more consumers cutting back or just ditching alcohol all together. Their main reason for doing so is to focus on mental health and well being.

-For those of you who enjoy working out you may have noticed this more and more. Online workout classes/live streams have become the go to for working out at home. This is due to the convenience and affordability.

-For those of you following along with celebrity gossip then you may have noticed the explosion of podcasts popping up left and right. You're not alone as you most likely noticed more and more celebrities are starting up podcasts. Followed by this an estimated 32 percent of Americans 12 and up listen to podcasts compared to the 26 percent in 2019.

-For those of you eco friendly and adapting to a greener living this is for you. More and more companies have made progress to reducing the different types of waste they produce. This ranges from using paper straws to reducing carbon footprints.

-For those of you who are into toys, toys have gotten more inclusive now with dolls offering more skin tones/races and more styles.

-A more noticeable one is the streaming revolution, with netflix, amazon prime, disney+ reaching new heights more and more streaming services are entering the game with more and more people joining.

-Some of you into cameras may have noticed this but more and more people have gotten into using disposable cameras. According to google trends at the time, more young people are fascinated by nostalgic devices and apparel.

-For those of you into gaming, the game awards had more views than the oscars with the gaming community growing substantially due to all the lockdowns.

-Lockdowns came into full effect from covid keeping people home. This caused many trends and habbits to explode in popularity such as gaming.

Hopefully you enjoy this small series and if you have anything that I've missed let me know. I was planning on including covid but that imo happened in 2019 not 2020.

Cheers!
