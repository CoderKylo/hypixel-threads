Untitled PokePaste
this is the closest I could think of for making a "good" eeveelution team.
I'm not really good at teambuilding nor do I play anything outside of OU and National Dex, so I'm not even sure how well it'll go.
Since physical eeveelutions suck and I couldn't really think of anything after Jolteon, I slapped on Flareon and Leafeon. Flareon will probably be the mon you dynamax most of the time as a means to set up sun so Leafeon's chlorophyll will proc but I honestly don't think Leafeon will do much anyway. That out of the way, Umbreon has foul play to kill things with high physical attack stats as well as utility moves such as toxic and wish to heal itself and provide support. Vaporeon was the choice for somewhat of a special wall and flip turn grants it momentum along with haze to get rid of boosts on opposing threats. Jolteon is there for damage because why not. I'm not even sure why Sylveon is there but it looked viable, could be a sweeper of some sorts I guess.

Enjoy
