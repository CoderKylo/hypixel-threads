Here's what I got to say,

That aside, who cares about a phone number, you can always change it and it reveals almost no info about you. First step for something like this is making a new email that is not linked to the old one in any way, and do it ON ANOTHER DEVICE. This is really really important as they sent you your discord token meaning they token logged you. Odds are you have a virus/rat on your pc so if you keep trying to change credentials they can sniff them out. However they are most likely trying to scare you as I'm 99% sure that they can't do anything with those details they sent other than to use them to blackmail you, which is most likely the intention or getting you to change credentials so they can find a way to sniff out more info on you.

Once you make a new email on another device, on that other device sign into discord and set it up for that new email. Also change the password and don't save it on your computer, write it down. That way the hacker or whoever has the details can't log in into your account. All they can do now is threaten you. And since you logged in and did the whole process on another device they have no way of knowing what that email is being used and what the new password is. In the meantime, disconnect your pc from wifi to prevent data being sent(if any), save all important files on a usb and install a new copy of windows. That way everything is deleted so if there was a rat it's no longer there. Then setup windows and whatnot. This may sound extreme but can save your butt in the future.

As for your questions, no they cannot do anything with a phone number, they can't intercept or do anything as they need your sim card which they don't have. With this scenerio you can most likely request to have your number changed and your carrier might do it for free. Also going back to what I said, he most likely token logged you which is quite common, it was most likely on your pc and not on your phone. To add to this malware on mobile phones is practically non existent unless you download it yourself. You also can't just hack someone's computer with an ip address, discord token and email, that's laughable and is 100% impossible.

If it helps you can also report the account to discord and also file a police report so if more stuff does happen you have something to go off of. Then just block the user and go on your way, they shouldn't be able to do much else.

However rest assured that he most likely does not have your password so really all he can do is scare you. if he did he would be using that to threaten you, which he isn't. Plus you need to enter your password to see your password on discord so this too indicates he does not know it. You should also block them, that way they can't really do anything. Who cares if he even leaks that info, you aren't a celebrity so no one will be interested in doing anything. However if people do decide to be crazy enough and show up at your door, having that police report open can really help you out.

That's my take on it. let me know if you have other questions. Just reset your pc, setup a new email for discord with a new password and you should be fine. And lesson learned to not buy accounts from discord as this whole thing has really really high odds of being a setup.
