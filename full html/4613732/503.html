You earn different forum titles by earning trophy points, which you earn for posting a certain amount of messages or earning a certain amount of positive reactions. You also earn some trophies for being on the forums for a certain amount of time. You can view a list of trophies that you can earn here.

The table below shows all of the different forum titles you can earn and the amount of trophy points you need to achieve them.
New Member0 Trophy Points
Member30 Trophy Points
Active Member75 Trophy Points
Well-Known Member150 Trophy Points
Dedicated Member250 Trophy Points
Forum Nerd450 Trophy Points
Forum Expert600 Trophy Points
Forum Veteran750 Trophy Points
Forum Professional1000 Trophy Points
Forum Legend1465 Trophy Points

If you want to see the possible ways to achieve each forum title, check out this thread.
