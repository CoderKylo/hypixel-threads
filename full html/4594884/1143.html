To be completely fair, I have created one of the top-grossing copypastas this forum has ever seen, and like most great inventions it was an accident. This is a good thing, right? No, no it isn't. Watching your own words get ridiculed by people genuinely hurts... And it's NOT funny. I try to laugh it off sometimes, and sometimes I hit a nerve as you know, but it truly hurts me. You have to realize that behind a screen is a sensible human being with feelings, just like you and me. The pit forums are, unfortunately, possibly the most toxic forum on the Hypixel network, and it really saddens me.

I was an innocent genuine person who liked to play the pit, but I wasn't as experienced as other players. I go on the forums for genuine advice and all I got were funny emojis and people making fun of me, my identity, and my bad English. It really hurts guys... Now, the worst thing is that there are so many copypastas made on the pit forums. Can you imagine how terrible every single person you made a copypasta out of feels? They must feel terrible, just so you can get a good laugh...

Take bhoppers in-game for example. They ruin the experience for many innocent people just so they could laugh at them. It's absolutely absurd. Do these bhoppers ever think about how much they hurt the player? How much lives they've taken away from a player? How much of a negative emotional impact they had on the player? How much they waste the player's item to get good items? No, because the world evolves around you, right? This should stop, and the same applies to the pit forums.

The pit and the pit forums have so much potential, but unfortunately, its toxic community is stopping it from reaching that potential. It's truly heartbreaking seeing what's happening to the pit forums and the amount of harassment, racism, homophobia, bigotry, fascism, and toxicity that happens on the pit forums. This could be a better place, but you guys must learn to be better as well.

Seriously guys. Make. This. Stop. This is not funny and it hurts a lot of people, including me. This could really be a better place but we must give it our all!
