For those of you who don't know who @Husket is, she is a super epic famous bedwars forumer who you should totally go follow by the way.

So I met Husket around late July/early August of this year. I had known her as a forumer who I looked up to for quite a while, but we had never actually spoken to each other till then. We became friends and started playing some bedwars, you know the usual.

Late August, I had plans to go on vacation to 2 states, Florida and (insert husket's state). When I told her and some of my other friends this, it was quite funny because we thought it would be hilarious if I met her while on my trip.

Well, that thought soon turned into an actual plan to meet her. She told me where she worked, and the original plan was to go into as many of those stores as possible and ask if she worked there lol. I honestly don't think I would've done that because it would be too awkward.

Then as I was on vacation, she told me the exact store she worked in, and the plan was I go meet her there during her work shift the next day.

So as planned, I went there, went through the drive thru, and I saw her at one of the windows. Now, did I say anything? Absolutely not. Hear me out. I had known her for like 2 weeks, 2 WEEKS. I would be nervous around a friend I had known for a year, let alone 2 weeks. It all sounded good in concept, but when I entered that drive thru I was like NOPE I'm out. I was way too nervous ok.

Yeah, she was disappointed, but listen I was way to scared then ok. If that story had happened now, when I knew her better? Yeah I probably would have at least spoken a bit. Still very awkward though.

As for husket not recognizing me by voice, I have no idea.

So that's the story of how I met a bedwars forumer in real life, but was too nervous to say anything! I'm such a great person aren't I
