I just realized that I've been on the server for 7 years. Wow, I've never thought that I could keep up playing hypixel for 7 years. It's been a journey. The journey is so fun and enjoyable.

On the year 2015, I used to main warlords, I chose to be a Paladin, I also play BedWars, Skywars, MM, and many more.

I also once played SkyClash, Battle Royale, and Crazy Walls too before it got deleted. Oof.

Before Skyblock released I used to main Pit. During that time. I don't have much time to actually play the game. I only got myself to Prestige II(not impressive) unfortunately.

Skyblock is my primary game now. I've played the game for 2 years and 4 months now. Damn skyblock is painful but I'm addicted to it.
This is my sb profile now: https://sky.shiiyu.moe/stats/dashsus/Lemon
