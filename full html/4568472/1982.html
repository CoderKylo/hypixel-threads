If you weren't already aware, Technoblade streamed "Minecraft But Viewers Control The Game [CHARITY EVENT]" which was a fundraiser event for SFA. During the stream, viewers could donate money to spawn in a random item or entity of their choice (prices varied) which is what made the stream as entertaining as it was.


Every time the fundraiser hit a new milestone, something new would happen during the stream. The spoiler below contains every milestone event.


Currently, Technoblade has raised over $325,000, and the number continues to climb as I write this.

Due to the vast amounts of donations flying through, as well as the chaotic nature of the stream, there was simply no time for anyone to pay much attention to the donations until later.

After the stream had finished, I headed over to Tiltify (the website Technoblade used for his fundraiser) to take a look at the details. What I wasn't expecting to find, but was also not too surprised about, was that Hypixel had donated $30,000 to the event.



Unfortunately, I joined the stream late, and just barely missed the time of the donation. I did, however, go back through the VOD, and found the time stamp for you guys.

The donation takes place at 1:36:13, summoning two withers as the 30k donation pushed the funds raised from 175k to 200k, which was a milestone.



The link to the official page and website used can be found in the spoiler below if you wish to take a look for yourself.

Tiltify - Made for Fundraisers

We give you the tools to engage with your donors and raise more for your charity
tiltify.com

This, of course, has not been the first time Hypixel has donated to Technoblade on-stream, but it has (to my knowledge) been the largest sum of money through a single donation.

I thought I would share this information here in the Off Topic forum as I wasn't sure how many people knew or would be interested to know. Whether or not it's relevant to you, I thought it was a little heart warming knowing this.

Thanks for giving this thread a read, and have a great day!
