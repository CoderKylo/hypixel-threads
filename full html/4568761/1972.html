first off disconnect from wifi as you don't want it to potentially spread onto other wifi devices. Then on another device download an antivirus like bitdefender and just make a fake email and sign up for the premium plan. Then I guess for this you'll need to enable wifi again but download bitdefender or an antivirus of your choice on your device from the usb stick and log in with the fake email. you don't want the virus to get your real email credentials. Then once you've got premium for 7 days run the scans and see what comes of it. If nothing is found then you're most likely better off saving your files that you value and completely formatting your system and starting from scratch, after all you don't want it collecting personal info and then selling it. Other good antiviruses are malware bytes, norton, avast, mcafee, kaspersky etc. Just see which one allow free trials without credit cards. Also try multiple antiviruses too. One might not always find all the viruses or might find something else the other one didn't find.
