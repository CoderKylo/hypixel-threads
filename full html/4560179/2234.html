So recently @StevenR8 has been talking about how great vanilla is so, I thought I’d give it a try.
No mods, Not even optifine. No resource packs.

Pls someone help

(If you want to know what I’m talking about and don’t know one of the mods you can check out my list
Part 1. First thoughts
When I first joined hypixel, my initial thoughts were
1. Wtf the scoreboard is so annoying, with my sidebar mod I usually have it scaled to at most half sized, it also removes the red numbers and the background.
2. Fps peaks higher then I expected. One of the first things I did was pull up the debug screen to check my fps, what I noticed was that it wasn’t all that different from my fps with mods, just slightly worse. HOWEVER, the fps is way less inconsistent, dropping much more then it does with mods so my conclusion is that optifine and patcher don’t make your fps peak that much higher, but rather stabilizes it, making it more consistent.

Part 2. Skywars
idk why I decided to do skywars as I never play it but I did it anyways yaaay
Game 1. The lack of togglesprint threw me off, making me take waaay longer then normal to loot. I got rushed and murdered.
Game 2. I was able to loot much quicker this time, being slightly more used to no togglesprint. I even bridged to mid but, the way I make bridges, I jump the last few blocks and don’t fully finish the bridge. However, I forgot to hit sprint because I wouldn’t normally have to with togglesprint aaand...I didn’t come close to clearing the jump
Game 3. This death is embarrassing. This time, everything worked fine and I got to mid, then I was attacked. I don’t remember what happened to the attacker, just that I was super low and needed to chug a gap. Now this is where I made a mistake. With old animations mods, normally it has a feature that lets you punch the ground (not actually digging) and eat food at the same time by holding both left and right click, mostly I just do it cuz it looks cool and uh...it kinda just became an addiction and habit. so back to the story, I tried to do that, but instead of doing the punch eat animation like it would with mods, it just started digging. An enemy walked up and murdered me easily since I didn’t have health.
Game 4. This game I actually did fairly decent. I was able to kill a couple players, even though it was much harder without my mods then with. I decided to move on after that since I almost never even play skywars

Part 3. Bedwars
Game 1. I got some resources and started bridging to the diamond generator to get to my first rush, once I got there I started speed bridging to my rush and fell (lack of old animations sneak animation). Once I respawned I got blocks again and ran back, this time they had built a bridge to where I fell. I fought them but realized halfway through I wasn’t sprinting and thus getting about zero hits in. Once I respawned they were on my bridge and started blocking it off (it was a staircase bridge). I bought a kb stick (fight me) and knocked them off my base when they dropped. I then started building along the side of my staircase bridge that was blocked off for the most part and at one point I had a one block gap with one block of elevation I thought dumbly I could clear so I jumped aaand forgot to sprint (relying on togglesprint again), I fell and died again. I eventually got their bed and final killed them. I then had my bed destroyed and I got killed by someone else.
Game 2. I don’t really remember this game, I think I got a headache halfway through because of playing in daytime when I normally use timechanger to change the time to night so it’s much darker and easier on my eyes (I always play at lowest brightness no matter what so that doesn’t change) at this point I was ready to be done playing vanilla and wanted to go lie down but first I had to do one more thing.

Part 4. Duels
I wanted to play duels because Bedwars and skywars aren’t super pvp oriented so I wanted to see how my pvp was affected by vanilla. I decided to play classic duels (rip my reaction score), I’m not gonna do a game by game for this just give a summary.
As mentioned earlier, I think the not stable FPS was really affect my ability to aim as it wasn’t as smooth as I was used to it being. Also I kept forgetting to sprint while in pvp since that would normally be automatic with togglesprint.

Part 5. Conclusion
Overall I think you can play without mods for the most part but it’s going to be way smoother and more enjoyable with them.
And also I think, dare I say it, that the added FPS is truly an advantage as I perform much worse with not as smooth FPS. I do think that there are some mods that I have in my mods folder that are not very needed for me, although they do make the game much more enjoyable, so here I give you the list of five mods that given the choice I would pick to keep
  1. Optifine, the fps boost is insane and idk what I would do without it
  2. Patcher, again FPS boost and also many useful things like Fulbright which also helps me to not get headaches as if I play some games without it I have to turn up my brightness which is much harder for my eyes
  3. Time changer, as said playing in night is much easier for my eyes as I get headaches a lot when playing in day
  4. Old animations I just love the look of old animations a lot better and while learning to speed bridge I’m learning it’s MUCH easier with the old sneak animation
  5. Togglesprint while technically I could get used to not using this one fairly easily I think it is helpful since it also makes you be able to sneak much faster and also frees up my pinkie which I sometimes assign a hot key for so I can hot key with that finger

I honestly don’t think I would play hypixel and Minecraft as a whole nearly as much if I didn’t use time changer because I would get a headache a lot of the time and think I would end up playing other games way more.
Alright you are welcome @StevenR8 i tried it out you happy now?

Kylomax said:
So recently @StevenR8 has been talking about how great vanilla is so, I thought I’d give it a try.
No mods, Not even optifine. No resource packs.

Pls someone help

(If you want to know what I’m talking about and don’t know one of the mods you can check out my list
Part 1. First thoughts
When I first joined hypixel, my initial thoughts were
1. Wtf the scoreboard is so annoying, with my sidebar mod I usually have it scaled to at most half sized, it also removes the red numbers and the background.
2. Fps peaks higher then I expected. One of the first things I did was pull up the debug screen to check my fps, what I noticed was that it wasn’t all that different from my fps with mods, just slightly worse. HOWEVER, the fps is way less inconsistent, dropping much more then it does with mods so my conclusion is that optifine and patcher don’t make your fps peak that much higher, but rather stabilizes it, making it more consistent.

Part 2. Skywars
idk why I decided to do skywars as I never play it but I did it anyways yaaay
Game 1. The lack of togglesprint threw me off, making me take waaay longer then normal to loot. I got rushed and murdered.
Game 2. I was able to loot much quicker this time, being slightly more used to no togglesprint. I even bridged to mid but, the way I make bridges, I jump the last few blocks and don’t fully finish the bridge. However, I forgot to hit sprint because I wouldn’t normally have to with togglesprint aaand...I didn’t come close to clearing the jump
Game 3. This death is embarrassing. This time, everything worked fine and I got to mid, then I was attacked. I don’t remember what happened to the attacker, just that I was super low and needed to chug a gap. Now this is where I made a mistake. With old animations mods, normally it has a feature that lets you punch the ground (not actually digging) and eat food at the same time by holding both left and right click, mostly I just do it cuz it looks cool and uh...it kinda just became an addiction and habit. so back to the story, I tried to do that, but instead of doing the punch eat animation like it would with mods, it just started digging. An enemy walked up and murdered me easily since I didn’t have health.
Game 4. This game I actually did fairly decent. I was able to kill a couple players, even though it was much harder without my mods then with. I decided to move on after that since I almost never even play skywars

Part 3. Bedwars
Game 1. I got some resources and started bridging to the diamond generator to get to my first rush, once I got there I started speed bridging to my rush and fell (lack of old animations sneak animation). Once I respawned I got blocks again and ran back, this time they had built a bridge to where I fell. I fought them but realized halfway through I wasn’t sprinting and thus getting about zero hits in. Once I respawned they were on my bridge and started blocking it off (it was a staircase bridge). I bought a kb stick (fight me) and knocked them off my base when they dropped. I then started building along the side of my staircase bridge that was blocked off for the most part and at one point I had a one block gap with one block of elevation I thought dumbly I could clear so I jumped aaand forgot to sprint (relying on togglesprint again), I fell and died again. I eventually got their bed and final killed them. I then had my bed destroyed and I got killed by someone else.
Game 2. I don’t really remember this game, I think I got a headache halfway through because of playing in daytime when I normally use timechanger to change the time to night so it’s much darker and easier on my eyes (I always play at lowest brightness no matter what so that doesn’t change) at this point I was ready to be done playing vanilla and wanted to go lie down but first I had to do one more thing.

Part 4. Duels
I wanted to play duels because Bedwars and skywars aren’t super pvp oriented so I wanted to see how my pvp was affected by vanilla. I decided to play classic duels (rip my reaction score), I’m not gonna do a game by game for this just give a summary.
As mentioned earlier, I think the not stable FPS was really affect my ability to aim as it wasn’t as smooth as I was used to it being. Also I kept forgetting to sprint while in pvp since that would normally be automatic with togglesprint.

Part 5. Conclusion
Overall I think you can play without mods for the most part but it’s going to be way smoother and more enjoyable with them.
And also I think, dare I say it, that the added FPS is truly an advantage as I perform much worse with not as smooth FPS. I do think that there are some mods that I have in my mods folder that are not very needed for me, although they do make the game much more enjoyable, so here I give you the list of five mods that given the choice I would pick to keep
  1. Optifine, the fps boost is insane and idk what I would do without it
  2. Patcher, again FPS boost and also many useful things like Fulbright which also helps me to not get headaches as if I play some games without it I have to turn up my brightness which is much harder for my eyes
  3. Time changer, as said playing in night is much easier for my eyes as I get headaches a lot when playing in day
  4. Old animations I just love the look of old animations a lot better and while learning to speed bridge I’m learning it’s MUCH easier with the old sneak animation
  5. Togglesprint while technically I could get used to not using this one fairly easily I think it is helpful since it also makes you be able to sneak much faster and also frees up my pinkie which I sometimes assign a hot key for so I can hot key with that finger

I honestly don’t think I would play hypixel and Minecraft as a whole nearly as much if I didn’t use time changer because I would get a headache a lot of the time and think I would end up playing other games way more.
Alright you are welcome @StevenR8 i tried it out you happy now?
