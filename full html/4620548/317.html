About December of 2020, I sucked at PVP and played on a laptop. I decided to switch to lunar and noticed an immediate improvement in my PVP. I was beating people I could never beat before and feeling great. I also remember it felt so much different to PVP on lunar also the graphics looked slightly different as well. I think this must've been due to the miss penalty being removed and lunar rendering differently but I'm not sure. Anyway, I got a PC at the beginning of 2021 that is a previously owned pre-built from 2012 that runs Windows 8.1. I have only ever used lunar of that PC until recently when I switched back to forge. I immediately noticed a few weird things. First of all the game was running at higher FPS than it did on lunar. I got 200-300 on lunar and now I get 300-700 (VERY optimized settings). The PVP also felt completely different. I was getting hits way faster and had little problems with hitreg. I would highly recommend forge+patcher+optifine for anyone that has a below-average PC or is getting hit reg problems with lunar. The difference was immeasurable for me.

Lunars graphics look different because of all their edits to Minecraft's source code and rendering engine(I think they use vulkan instead of openGL, but I'm not sure, vulkan is faster and utilizes cpu/GPU cores better, plus I found a vulkan-1.dll file in it's directory, so maybe)

And now for the hitreg, I personally think it's more of the person than the client sometimes, some say it has better hitreg, others say it has worse than vanilla or optifine)
