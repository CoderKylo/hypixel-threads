Disclaimer- before i start a lot of this is based off memory's of stuff that happened a long time ago so sorry if I miss anyone or stuff is inaccurate

I first joined hypixel in September of 2017 yes i know this post is late shut up it took me ages to get motivation to write
I don't really remember much about my early days on the network however i know at the time I was and still am a big pvper and played a now kind of dead factions server.

The Pit
This is the first notable part of my story i played pit for a total of about 300hours yes i know that isn't much in reality but i am still in school and this was pre-covid so no playing during class I slowly grinded my way to ~prestige 7or8 then would quit the pit(for now) when skyblock released

Skyblock
well i have some reasonable stats including former number12 in iron

now i am sat at nearly 1k hours 800m nw(not including exotics) and 40skill average

Return of the combat
After mildly getting bored with skyblock due to being stuck in midgame i decided to go back to yes the hypixel pit aka "The Oldest Anarchy GameMode On Hypixel" so during lockdown and bit before I got all the way to prestige 12 were i currently sit. After reaching prestige12 in April I started grinding duels from under 1k wins to nearly 5k where i am now during this time i also grinding up to 10skywars stars

Guilds
A few small guilds being Carpenters-now disbanded
NF- i think its disbanded idk i remember rm_rf_slash was in it before leaving for a endgame guild
Hypixel guardians by far my favourite guild was in it for over a year only left recently for DDen (thinking about rejoining tbh)


(Hypixel Guardians ^ )
Now that my hypixel story is done time for some cooler stuff mainly just flexing on my friends online and irl

I'm 5'9 at 16 imagine being short couldn't be me cough Millie cough
I weight about 52kg yeah I know I weight basically nothing
I am currently am studying: Politics, Economics, Business and Psychology
i get like 2-3 assignments a week yeah my friends in the same year get 10 so LLLLLL

Now I'm your average hypixel teenage nerd you know the few friends quiet kid guy well fellow nerds I have a motivating story (I guess)
so basically i went up a school in the system this year i think you Americans call it high school in my area we have 3 mains schools for my age
i went to one literally all of my friends went to a different one which I wasn't able to get into cause of my grades obviously very sadge about this after about 2weeks I go into a building with someone I know from my previous school 1week later i now have a whole new set of friends and I still do talk to my old friends online i was planning to meetup with them during my half term (started today) but i got left on "let me see when I'm free" and no response guess more time for hypixel so yes fellow nerds always remember when you hit rock bottom the only way is up

Thanks for reading nerds see you next year for my 5years

-RedKite17

Edit: some extra screenshots i found and spelling/grammer
