Alot of people like lego figures. sometimes they're easy to get, other may cost you a pretty penny.


as a lego collector, ive amased a few figures that cost an arm and a leg to get. some on purpose for a complete collection, others by accident, and they're accended in value.

#15
Number 15 presents us with Agen Kolar, from palpatines arrest. A side character that had barely any screentime, but looks cool af. his exclusive head piece makes him a desirable figure for any star wars collector, and thats why he'll set you back $46 used.


#14

At #14 we have a figure from 2003. Princess leia in her cloud city attire. Many people know from reading previous threads that the cloud city collection is one of the rarest things you can own as a lego collector, so expect to see a few of these on here. I payed $500 for the whole cloud city lot, so thats $71 dollars per figure. This one is worth around $46 used, so i maybe overpayed, but when we look at the others, i dont think i did personally.


#13

#13 presents us with a figure from the 2012 wave of star wars sets. (expect to see alot of star wars). Specifically from the old republic range, and thats darth malgus. a completley exclusive figure in the sith fury class interceptor, with a brand new breathing apparatus piece and amazing looks, its easy to see why he costs $49 used. Also added onto the fact that he was relatively unknown in 2012, but has become more prominent with the book releases on him, so people really want him.


#12

At the 12th spot we have the cloud city boba. No, not THE cloud city boba, the boba from the 2018 cloud city set, betrayal on cloud city. an amazing upgrade from the 2003 varient, with printing, well everywhere. he's such a beauty to behold, and is honestly one of my favourite figures. this masterpiece of a figure will blow your wallet away with $52 lost, so be prepared. he's a valuable man with a very particular set of skills.


#11

At 11, we have the slimy grima wormtongue from LOTR. Coming in the biggest set of the theme, and tallest, he is quite obscure, and lacks printing, but looks incredibly for the $52 you need to pay for him.


#10

Just barely scrapping the top 10 we have velma from scooby doo! bet you didnt see that coming. Almost being the same price as the set she came in, the haunted mansion, being almost $60, she has an exclusive hair piece and amazing prints all around, and is quite an expensive figure for your mysterious collection of scooby doo figures.


#9

At number 9, we have the clown prince of crime himself, gotham citys #1 criminal, the joker. specifically the dark knight version from the 2014 tumbler. He laughs as you fork out $65 for this menacing clown, beating you once again.


#8

At the eigth spot, we have another old republic figure, specifically the unnamed jedi knight from the jedi defender class cruiser in 2013. he's $70 to get your hands on, more expensive than the actual set at retail, and is quite the stranger for marvelous printing.


#7
Speaking of marvelous, we have the leader of clan vizla at #7. Such a beauty as a character and a figure. This masked man sets us back around $70 again, but its fully worth it. He came out in the $50 mandalorian fighter from 2012.


#6
Probably the coolest figure on the list, We have another mandalorian. No, not boba. Darth revan. The mighty sith lord. He was a may 4th promo in 2014, and costed you around $50 to get your hands on. Now he's 80, and truly desireable, however, he does fetch up to $120, for a good condition version.

#5
Number 5 gives us the amazing captain rex, specifically the p2 varient. He isnt as cool as cody imo, (we need phase 2 cody!!!), but he's definately really cool. He'll cost you $90 minimum, but can go for 150. The bloody kama is $40 alone! its strange for a figure from a $30 set in 2013 to be so expensive, but here he is.

#4
Number 4 gives us the rebellion hero Lando Calrisian from 2018. A massive upgrade from the 2003 version, and has a really desirable cape and printing. Not worth the $90 imo, but definately a cool character i accidentally came across.

#3
Speaking of lando, we have the 2003 varient from cloud city. Here we make the jump from $90 to $230, with an exclusive cape and body, and definately one of the must haves for the cloud city collection. i already mentioned i payed for the lot of them for $500, so this one rounds out at an amazing deal.

#2
Speaking of cloud city, we have the 2003 varient of luke. he's the same price as lando, so you gotta pick, but i honestly prefer this figure more because he only has one exclusive part. the legs. I have around 5 of this figure without the legs, because they're $225 each.

#1
We all saw it coming. one of the most expensive retail figures from a retail set ever. THE GIGA CHAD, 2003 BOBA FETT. Arm printing, leg printing, you get it all with this bad boy. except he's only $840. Yeah. Not alot of people own this bad boy, but im proud to say he's been safe in my collection for the past 3 years in achrilic glass. My varient is mint condition obviously, so he's not leaving that case any time soon.



Thats the list! Lots of big numbers thrown around, but people ask me this all the time so i think it should be a seperate thread. Thanks for reading!

(Disclaimer: I didnt buy these figures for their prices listed. I bought them all retail apart from the cloud city figures.)

(Disclaimer 2: None of these photos are mine. they're taken from brickset, but i do own them all, i just cant be bothered to go find them lol.)
