Hey! Things have been happening over the last few weeks, so i thought id share.


Figure displays mark 3 are in production. I went a whole new route with these ones. Mark 2 was the same idea as a picture frame, and were quite fragile. Mark 3 has its structure inside the wall, as well as being bolted down in multiple areas. They take a bit of time to do each, but we’re currently sitting at 2/7 displays remodelled and complete.


Smaller ones have also received the upgrade, but are slightly different to the other ones. I have 2 types done, with a hanging varient as well as a picture frame/desk varient, with this one being able to sit upright in a desk.

Lastly, some more Lego is being moved back in from the storage lot.

There’s creator, ninjago box 1, Jurassic world, and the Death Star from top to bottom. Lego movies also back in, which is in the left. I’ve also had the tumbler, simpsons house and the quick-e-mart back in too.

Ok bye
