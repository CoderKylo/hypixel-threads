So commentary channels have been a thing in youtuber for a while and come to think of it, their videos are kinda lazy tbh. Like they just recorded a gameplay for 10 minutes, find a topic that is trending for a while, make the commentary, edit it and the post it. BOOM they get thousands of views for basically talking about a topic. Some commentary channels are good actually, some people put a ton of effort on their commentary videos like Spoctor and DannyGonzalez. What makes their videos special is they make it funny and use some sort of face. For Danny's videos he uses his camera to record himself explaining things which gives more effect to explanation. Spoctor's videos have a digital drawings with different emotions for each drawing to express more thought, that's what makes spoctor original. For danny's videos he uses his facecam for commentaries, for spoctor's videos he uses digital drawings with different emotions which makes it stand out. Most commentary channels are literally bland and just copy and pasting which make their content so lazy tbh.

TLDR: Most Commentary channels are lazy, they are just copy and pasting, they are popular because they just tag on popular trends unless your commentary videos stand out and have your original twist to the commentary unlike e the other commentary channels that just put background gameplay + voice recording.
