depends what you consider as a vpn, a vpn itself is a virtual private network that reroutes your connection. Using a public one will run you into trouble because that ip could have been banned or that ip will trigger a security ban. However using your own vpn is mostly allowed because you can connect from your own device to your own device. Say you're on vacation in Cuba and bring your laptop to play hypixel. You can use your own vpn to connect your laptop to your desktop at home and play the game like that. That would be allowed as hypixel technically sees you logged in from your desktops location and the ip you logged in from is your own so there was most likely no malicious activity. However this is starting to stretch into a vps.
