Chapter 1

9 years ago on the coast of Eyton


Captain Jaxin, the captain of the feared Jaxin pirates who were the most feared pirates in the southern ocean of Ewia, was sitting in his cabin, when his right hand man, Jenkin Cotts entered the cabin.
“Sir, we’ve found a stowaway hiding on top of one of the crow nests,” said Jenkin.
Jaxin said,“How did they get on? Were none of you looking? You numbskulls. Are they a government dog?” The words fluttered out of his mouth like invisible daggers.
Jenkins said cooly, “Well the shocking thing is, he's a boy around the age of 6.”
There was a moment of silence as Jaxin took this into consideration. “Bring him in,” Jaxin said, “I want to have a word with him.”
…
Jaxin analyzed the boy that stood before him, He had Blackish brown hair and his eyes were a dull shade of grey. His clothes were ragged and full of holes. He wore a hat with the honoro insigma on it and he looked as if he hadn’t eaten or drank in days.
Jaxin asked“,Boy what's your name?” The boy stared down to the ground. His hands started to shake and did not give an answer, after a couple of minutes Jaxin asked once again, “Boy what is your name, otherwise I will turn you into a corpse and use you as a figurehead. Hearing this the boy finally said “It's Dan.”
“I’m assuming you have honoro powers?”, Jaxin asked casually.
“Y-yes sir, at least I think so” Dan quivered.
Jaxin said, “Let's test that theory shall we?” Without warning he took out a sword and chopped Dan’s arms clean off. Dan howled and collapsed from the pain, blood gushing out from where his arms were, but after a couple of seconds his skin started healing and slowly his elbows, his palms, and finally his fingers regrew.
“The bastard wasn’t lying,” Jenkins chuckled in amusement.
Jaxin wiped the blood off his blade, He then said, “We could always use an honoro, take him to the C-deck and get him changed and showered up, then we’ll have a talk at dinner.”
…
Dan woke up and hit his head on the bed frame. Dan winced, rubbing the back of his head. He put out his hands in front of him and stared. He didn’t believe that he had powers, he had seen with his own eyes his hands chopped off but here they were in front of him.
“Ah so you’re finally awake.”
Dan looked to the side, to see a man who looked like he was electrocuted several times. “Captain’s waiting for ya.” best come down to the mess hall. They walked towards the mess hall and several minutes later Dan found himself at Jaxin’s table while the rest of his main fleet ,all 661 of them, sat around.
Jaxin said while cutting a piece of steak, “So how exactly did you end up on the crows nest?”
Dan’s palms started to sweat, “Well I was with my father at the docks, and my father told me to look for a Waloop night fish when I was knocked in the head by a lead pipe and shoved into the sea,” Dan started fidgeting with his hands, “I can’t swim and your ship happened to be nearby so as the anchor was pulling up I grabbed onto it and held on to the side of it. I decided to go to the crows nest to hide because I only realized this was a pirate ship after I grabbed the anchor.”
Jaxin asked, “How long were you up there?”
Dan said nervously, “about 5 da-”. Jaxin slammed the table and food spilt over.
“Who was on duty 5 days ago on the crow nest,” Jaxin asked slowly. Silence, not one person dared speak. “WHO WAS IT,” Jaxin roared.
A scared crewmate stood forward. He stammered “It-it was-as me captain.”
Jaxin said very quietly in such a way that sent chills up your spine, “How did you let a 6 year old child sneak aboard the ship without notice until today?”
The crewmember stuttered, “Bu-but I didn-”. The words were silenced with a bang of a sniper rifle being shot. The man hunched over and didn’t get back up. Jaxin slowly sat back down. He then said, “Now Dan, I’ll offer you a deal, join our crew and if you don’t, we kill you right on the spot. Isn’t that just swell?”
Dan knowing he had no other choice had to agree to this reluctantly replied, “Yes… captain.”
Jaxin smiled, “Good however”, His smile turned back to a frown, “If you try to leave our crew or betray us, we will know, Jenkins implanted a spell on your right eye that will tell us of any immediate betrayal or escape attempts, got that?”
“Yes sir,”Dan replied miserably.
Jaxin said, “Glad we’re on the same page, now for your first assignment.”




7 years ago
Chapter 2
Dan is 8 years old now, He has been a member of the Jaxin pirates for 2 years now and has honed and developed his skills. His hair has grown longer and he now wears a lightweight starfall chestpiece and standard military pants. Dan still wears his cap and his presence is different from the one two years prior. He also now wields a spear with a aquatic tinted blade. He is currently returning from the East to form an alliance between the Masked ones , the Jaxin pirates, and the Sickle pirates.
The radio crackled on his tiny sailboat, Dan reached for it and answered, “hello, this is commander 8 Dan speaking, What do you need Commander 1 Jenkins?”
“Ah Dan, we have a mission for you, come back to the headquarters.
“Understood”, Dan said before closing off his transmission.
3 days later …
Dan tied his boat to the dock. As he was walking through the gateway he sensed something wrong and dodged to the right, a split second after he did so a fire bolt whizzed past and grazed his left ear.
“So I see your ability has improved since the last time we met”,said the man who shot the bolt as he stepped out of the shadows.
“Of course Commander 5 Hordgin, did you really think i spent the last 9 months being diplomatic?” Dan said in a monotonic voice, “Lets go”.
“I haven’t seen you in a year and all that nervousness is gone from your earlier days,’Hordgin chuckled at himself.
As they continued walking down the steps, Dan said,”Of course, that’s what killing people does to you”. They finally reached the end of the staircase. Hordgin and Dan entered the room, finding Jaxin, Jenkins, and the corpse of a recently killed prisoner hanging from the wall,
“Ah Dan and Hordgin, you’re finally here for your assignment, sit down, sit down,” Jaxin said smiling sickly as he threw a sword at the corpse and laughed as more blood gushed out. Taking out a golfer Jaxin took out a report and gave it to Dan and Hordgin, he then said,” We recently received reports from Jakian city that the 20th King’s son is lonely and needs a friend, this is our perfect chance to assassinate him.”
Dan asked “Captain, may I ask why I’m needed for this mission and why do we need to kill the king’s son?”
Jaxin replied,”You’re the same age as the king’s son and if you become his playmate you can get closer to him and kill him. Hordgin here is only here to escort you as there will be S classed and A classed warriors defending the heir. Killing the son would mean the end of the bloodline and it would demoralize the king enough for us to storm the capital and kill him.
Jenkins took out a dagger,he said “ Hide this in your belt department. The son is a ewian, they are naturally immune to flesh wounds and require many poisons to kill.”
Dan said,”When will we be leaving?’
Jaxin replied,“right now. Go along now I still need to question the other prisoners for their stash whereabouts.” With that Dan and Hordgin headed off to Jakian city on a dragofly to prepare for the assassination of the heir.
Chapter 3
“Your son is going to be his majesty’s playmate correct,” the guard asked. The guard started scanning Hordgin for any weapons.
“Yes I paid a lot for my son to have this opportunity, he doesn’t have a lot of friends”
, Hordgin said.
Dan said in a shrill voice, “I can’t wait to meet his lord”.

All of the sudden, the boy was right in front of him.
The boy said, “Hi, nice to meet you! My name is Bob, how about you?”
The guard said, “I’ll leave you two in here and stand guard outside”. He closed the doors.
“Well?”, Bob asked, his entire body trembling as if he was radiating with excitement.
Dan finally said, “My name is Dan nice to meet you,” Dan said holding out his hand. His other hand grabbing the knife. Before he could take it out however, Bob said “Let me show you something” grabbed Dan's hand and Dan lost his grip on the knife and it fell to the floor but they were already several meters away where it wouldn’t be noticed.
The next couple days, Dan tried and tried again to kill the prince, trying to chuck a brick on his head, getting a vial of acid but accidentally burning himself. The prince didn’t notice these attempted murders and instead forced Dan to play with him. Gradually Dan felt the joy of being a child again grasp his heart. Despite his desperate attempts to brush it off. By the end of the second week, Dan had forgotten about the murder attempts and gradually grew to accept Jake as a friend. However on the night before the last day, as he was sleeping, his eye started throbbing and he heard the voice of Jenkins, “WHY HAVEN’T YOU FINISHED THE JOB KILL HIM.”
Dan woke up from his sleep, he suddenly remembered he had to kill Bob. His new best friend, why? With a heavy heart, he took out the knife, and tried to stab Bob. Before he could however, Bobstood up.
Bob said, “I knew it, the second you dropped the knife I knew you were an assassin.”
He turned towardsdan, “Why do you want to kill me? I thought we were friends, you stopped trying after a week ago. Why pick it up again?”
Dan started sobbing. He couldn’t kill his only friend. His eye started burning and Hordgin appeared. He said, “It seems you can’t kill the boy so I will have to kill him myself”
Approaching the boy, he started choking Jake and grabbed a poisoned dagger out. As he was about to stab the boy, Dan sprung into action. He did a somersault and kicked Hordgin knocking him out.Hordgin turned on his nightmare eyes. Dan tried to punch him but got dodged. Hordgin let go of Bob,who was gasping for breath. Hordgin hearing the guards said “frick I got caught.” and ran away. Jake asked “Are you ok,Dan?”
No, I’m not, I have to die now, Dan said emotionless. Jaxin will find out I betrayed him. “You don’t have to , we can-”
“SHUT UP”, Dan shouted, “You don’t know the things I've been through. I have killed, robbed, destroyed lives, I have to die.” Without warning he took the poisoned dagger and started stabbing his right eye. “I WANT TO DIE, I WANT TO DIE, I WANT TO DI-”. His hand was stopped. “Stop it, we can get you some help, you can still be my friend.”
Dan thought I can have a friend? My entire life I didn’t believe in friendship. Then I had a brief taste of it and it was gone. If there's one thing this world can let me have….
Dan said, shaking and stumbling, “I wa-want to live”. The world faded to blackness around him.
Chapter 5 the end
Dan woke up in an infirmary with a bandage over his right eye. Bob noticed this and said, “You're awake!” hugging Dan. Dan asked confused, “What happened?”
Bob said, “Your eye had all this jizz oozing out and they had to remove the poison. They removed the curse effect on your eye and whoever you were working for should think you are dead. Thing is though, your eye will never grow back because of the curse and poison.”
“Shouldn’t I be in prison?”, Dan asked.
Bob replied, “I got my father to pardon you, in exchange you’ll be my bodyguard.”
Dan, thinking for a while, decided that was better than being imprisoned.
“What about Hordgin?” Dan asked.
Bob said, “He committed ******* before he could get cornered, guess he knew he could never escape.”
“Yeah,” Dan said turning towards the window, a bright future ahead for him
Present day
“Dan, we heard news of Goblins attacking, we’ll need your input on this,” a now 15 year old Bob said, lifting up the flap.
Dan now a commander said, “coming”
The boys headed out to the palace now friends

“I haven’t seen you in a year and all that nervousness is gone from your earlier days,’Hordgin chuckled at himself.
As they continued walking down the steps, Dan said,”Of course, that’s what killing people does to you”. They finally reached the end of the staircase. Hordgin and Dan entered the room, finding Jaxin, Jenkins, and the corpse of a recently killed prisoner hanging from the wall,
“Ah Dan and Hordgin, you’re finally here for your assignment, sit down, sit down,” Jaxin said smiling sickly as he threw a sword at the corpse and laughed as more blood gushed out. Taking out a golfer Jaxin took out a report and gave it to Dan and Hordgin, he then said,” We recently received reports from Jakian city that the 20th King’s son is lonely and needs a friend, this is our perfect chance to assassinate him.”
Dan asked “Captain, may I ask why I’m needed for this mission and why do we need to kill the king’s son?”
Jaxin replied,”You’re the same age as the king’s son and if you become his playmate you can get closer to him and kill him. Hordgin here is only here to escort you as there will be S classed and A classed warriors defending the heir. Killing the son would mean the end of the bloodline and it would demoralize the king enough for us to storm the capital and kill him.
Jenkins took out a dagger,he said “ Hide this in your belt department. The son is a ewian, they are naturally immune to flesh wounds and require many poisons to kill.”
Dan said,”When will we be leaving?’
Jaxin replied,“right now. Go along now I still need to question the other prisoners for their stash whereabouts.” With that Dan and Hordgin headed off to Jakian city on a dragofly to prepare for the assassination of the heir.
Chapter 3
“Your son is going to be his majesty’s playmate correct,” the guard asked. The guard started scanning Hordgin for any weapons.
“Yes I paid a lot for my son to have this opportunity, he doesn’t have a lot of friends”
, Hordgin said.
Dan said in a shrill voice, “I can’t wait to meet his lord”.
The guard finished checking and said, “Right, come back to retrieve your son by next month.” Dan was brought into the prince chambers and there sat on a boy around the same age as him with a bored expression on his face. He had silky smooth black hair, and wore royal clothing. When he noticed Dan, the boy’s expression changed to delight and he jumped from the floor leaving an indent behind. All of the sudden, the boy was right in front of him.
The boy said, “Hi, nice to meet you! My name is Bob, how about you?”
The guard said, “I’ll leave you two in here and stand guard outside”. He closed the doors.
“Well?”, Bob asked, his entire body trembling as if he was radiating with excitement.
Dan finally said, “My name is Dan nice to meet you,” Dan said holding out his hand. His other hand grabbing the knife. Before he could take it out however, Bob said “Let me show you something” grabbed Dan's hand and Dan lost his grip on the knife and it fell to the floor but they were already several meters away where it wouldn’t be noticed.
The next couple days, Dan tried and tried again to kill the prince, trying to chuck a brick on his head, getting a vial of acid but accidentally burning himself. The prince didn’t notice these attempted murders and instead forced Dan to play with him. Gradually Dan felt the joy of being a child again grasp his heart. Despite his desperate attempts to brush it off. By the end of the second week, Dan had forgotten about the murder attempts and gradually grew to accept Jake as a friend. However on the night before the last day, as he was sleeping, his eye started throbbing and he heard the voice of Jenkins, “WHY HAVEN’T YOU FINISHED THE JOB KILL HIM.”
Dan woke up from his sleep, he suddenly remembered he had to kill Bob. His new best friend, why? With a heavy heart, he took out the knife, and tried to stab Bob. Before he could however, Bobstood up.
Bob said, “I knew it, the second you dropped the knife I knew you were an assassin.”
He turned towardsdan, “Why do you want to kill me? I thought we were friends, you stopped trying after a week ago. Why pick it up again?”
Dan started sobbing. He couldn’t kill his only friend. His eye started burning and Hordgin appeared. He said, “It seems you can’t kill the boy so I will have to kill him myself”
Approaching the boy, he started choking Jake and grabbed a poisoned dagger out. As he was about to stab the boy, Dan sprung into action. He did a somersault and kicked Hordgin knocking him out.Hordgin turned on his nightmare eyes. Dan tried to punch him but got dodged. Hordgin let go of Bob,who was gasping for breath. Hordgin hearing the guards said “frick I got caught.” and ran away. Jake asked “Are you ok,Dan?”
No, I’m not, I have to die now, Dan said emotionless. Jaxin will find out I betrayed him. “You don’t have to , we can-”
“SHUT UP”, Dan shouted, “You don’t know the things I've been through. I have killed, robbed, destroyed lives, I have to die.” Without warning he took the poisoned dagger and started stabbing his right eye. “I WANT TO DIE, I WANT TO DIE, I WANT TO DI-”. His hand was stopped. “Stop it, we can get you some help, you can still be my friend.”
Dan thought I can have a friend? My entire life I didn’t believe in friendship. Then I had a brief taste of it and it was gone. If there's one thing this world can let me have….
Dan said, shaking and stumbling, “I wa-want to live”. The world faded to blackness around him.
Chapter 5 the end
Dan woke up in an infirmary with a bandage over his right eye. Bob noticed this and said, “You're awake!” hugging Dan. Dan asked confused, “What happened?”
Bob said, “Your eye had all this jizz oozing out and they had to remove the poison. They removed the curse effect on your eye and whoever you were working for should think you are dead. Thing is though, your eye will never grow back because of the curse and poison.”
“Shouldn’t I be in prison?”, Dan asked.
Bob replied, “I got my father to pardon you, in exchange you’ll be my bodyguard.”
Dan, thinking for a while, decided that was better than being imprisoned.
“What about Hordgin?” Dan asked.
Bob said, “He committed ******* before he could get cornered, guess he knew he could never escape.”
“Yeah,” Dan said turning towards the window, a bright future ahead for him
Present day
“Dan, we heard news of Goblins attacking, we’ll need your input on this,” a now 15 year old Bob said, lifting up the flap.
Dan now a commander said, “coming”
The boys headed out to the palace now friends

Somewhere on the southern ocean…..
“Can’t believe that traitor had to go and die,”Jaxin mumbled, “We could have formed alliances with the other crews faster, but now,” he started chuckling, “The time is coming when the 4 pirate kings will take over the world.”
