Hello my good amigos, today I am hitting 10,000 messages AND 15,000 reactions. This is because I happen to be #1. Owen #1. Terminator #1. Arnold Schwarzenegger #1. Danny DeVito #1. You #1. We all are #1.

Anyways, I’ve been relatively active (although I’ve had inactivity waves at times) for over two years, since around August/September of 2019. In my time, I’ve seen a lot of things, some good, some bad, and some ugly. Overall, I’ve thoroughly enjoyed my time on the forums, even though there have been a lot of bad things, it’s overall been a positive experience. Now it’s time for thank yous.

We’ll start with staff.

@TorWolf - the first staff I really saw and talked to (note: said hi to) on the forums. A really nice guy, was a mod when I joined and now is an admin in Customer Support.

@Teddy - Also a mod when I first joined the forums, Teddy has risen through the ranks and is now Community Manager. Forum moderation has improved drastically since I joined. In 2019, alting, like spamming, trolls, were all rampant. Now, trolls are still a thing, but they’re handled better and faster. I know Teddy isn’t the only staff, so kudos to the rest of the forum mods as well.

@Cecer - My favorite staff member (sorry other staff) Cecer regularly interacts with the community, cracks jokes, and is just a really fun guy to talk to in general. Always gives useful tips, especially when it comes to map rotations.

Now it’s on to normal users.

@greninja - One of my biggest inspirations to continue the forums. You helped me more then you’ll ever know, with everything from irl advice, to getting better at bedwars. You were always one of the only forumers who could keep a cool head in heated situations and have consistently good advice on how to deal with trolls. There were several times where I almost quit the forums, and I would have if it wasn’t for you. It still hurts to think about you and watch your videos. I’ll miss you every day for the rest of my life.

@Lemon - One of the few forumers who was active when I was new and is still active now. I feel like lemon and I started to be active at the same time, I was more active at first, but as time went on I became less active and lemon more. Not to sound egotistical, but I also think we both make good threads, and they often cover similar topics.

Thank you to my guild, especially the staff. I’ve built up a pretty good guild, but I never would have been able to do it without the amazing people who’ve been at my side, both currently and in the past.
I would also like to thank the actual members in my guild, especially those who have been around for a long time. Your support means the world to me.

There are a lot of people who mean a lot to me who I didn’t tag, people like Kift, Crafter, Amoura, and many many more. You know who you are. If you’ve had a positive impact on the forums, thank you, I appreciate it. The world needs more people like you.

With this, I’ll wrap it up. I probably won’t be as active as I have been the last few weeks, but I’ll still be around. I love you guys, stay safe.

Hasta la vista, baby

Owen
