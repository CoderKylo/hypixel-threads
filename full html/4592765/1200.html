stories

1. the bus incident

after a long day at school, i got onto the bus which would take me home where i can procrastinate and watch youtube and then grind homework a few hours after. as i was getting on, i wrote my name in the paper thing and walked to find a seat. i was editing something on a bench after 9th period so i didn’t get on the bus until a few mins before it started to leave. this meant that there were no seats available.

except

there was the 2nd seat on the left in the front. it was right under the air conditioner which sounds nice in theory, right? but it was the only available seat for a reason. me, knowing the **** that’s bout to go down, sits on that seat. i was hesitating on taking my umbrella out because i was pretty sure i was going to need it.

after a few mins, the bus started to leave. things were going smoothly. until…

oml it ****ing exploded i kid you not. i had literal torrents of water pour right down onto me. i thought i was going to drown holy ****. worst part is that it didn’t only affect me, but also affected the girl behind me. the water ricocheted off of the back of my seat into what the girl was working on with her laptop or something.

anyways, the driver looked in the mirror absolutely stunned at what was occurring.

me, being the victim and idiot of the situation, surrendered and accepted defeat to the water until i realized it’s just water, not my life on the line. it soon stopped after drenching me and the girl’s laptop.

maybe my antisocial self will ask to sit next to someone next time who knows.



2. accidentally blasted music in class (good music tho)

it was time for 4th period and i was already tired of existing. it was a chill period. we were working on a project deadline and were given permission to listen to music while we work. taking this opportunity, i finished the notes i was making and took my phone and headphones out.

now, i’m not the average school student who listens to basic music and stuff like that. i listen to japanese music by karania and other artists, which @Ewianking introduced me too (big thanks to them).

anyways, i take my phone out and pull up spotify unaware of the fact i haven’t plugged my headphones in. i turn the volume up to around halfway, more or less and press play on the playlist.

omfg, i was so embarrased when i realized i didn’t plug my headphones in. it was only half a second since i pressed play to when i SPAMMED THAT DECREASE VOLUME BUTTON.

most kids were listening to documentaries while i was doing a book so i don’t think too many people have been blessed by such amazing music.

the funny thing is, is that this actually happened 2 mins before it happened to me, to someone else in the class.



3. biggest motivation burst in history

after struggling on a research project for the past 2 hours, i basically lost all motivation to do work. i sat miserably at my desk staring at the source i was working on. i had 2 projects due that night which i was close to finishing, but still far from being done.

but, i had the brilliant idea to ask my friends for motivation.

one of my friends had said, “imagine a cute anime boy at the turn in button,” jokingly. but it actually worked.

oml, i never worked so fast in my life. i finished that source in 7 minutes compared to the 2 hours before. i then wrote a detailed summary of that article within 10 minutes. “holy ****,” i thought. i then pulled up the source list i had to make a bibliography on, which i did faster than the speed of sound.

it may be weird, but it worked. so, if you have any motivation issues, just imagine something like what i thought of and you‘ll be literally unstoppable.



thank you for reading. i’ll probably make this a series depending on my new form of motivation.
