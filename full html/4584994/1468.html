spread your arms and legs in a mutated T shape to gain air drag and slow the fall slightly.

do not land in water. because of insane surface tension of water landing on water is basically same as landing on concrete.
best places to land are on top of trees, heavily snowed area, and swamps.
you can consider yourself to be gone if you are parachuting in the middle of the ocean.

switch to a standing position with your toes outstretched just before you hit the ground. land on your toes first, just be aware that both of your legs will shatter and you will feel intense pain when landing.
