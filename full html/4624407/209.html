Title explains it. But the reason I'm asking this, if you want to know, was surprisingly after watching a LTT Video. "Was Windows Vista that BAD?" and the conclusion they got to, was because there was not enough transparency on what changed and the launch was more but confusing. But the features Vista implemented shaped the future of Windows.

But less about Windows you can break cause they are made of glass and more about full on battles with swords.

1.9 was, at least for me, nothing more than what Windows Vista wanted to do. Changing the PvP Mechanism to make it newer and fresh. They also made changes to The End and added Elytras which was super cool. The 1.9 Update was something Mojang saw was good for the game itself, but the players reacted to it the same way people reacted to Windows Vista, because those players found themselves in a new version that would require them to change their whole PvP Mechanics.

I'm gonna be honest here. Humans have this certain tendency to never change to something new and adapt. We never want to leave our comfort zone and I feel that is what made 1.9 PvP look this bad.

But what about the new PvP. The one from the secret snapshots? Well, they changed a lot of stuff again, but they haven't removed something which may be a problem to some. The Attack Cooldown. Although they reduced it along with the weapon damage, some want the attack Cooldown DEAD, but, honestly, I don't.

Look, we all know we are supposed to laugh at Bedrock and it's servers, but the most fun I had while playing SkyWars, was actually on the Hive. You can still combo there, but the attack Cooldown exists, but in a way that if you click while it is still on it doesn't count of smth. The ping is also smaller there, so if you have Bedrock, go and test the PvP there, trust me, it's good.

I feel like a overhaul of the PvP Mechanics would be GREAT when they eventually release.

But what do you think? Let me know
Best regards, Nyxw
