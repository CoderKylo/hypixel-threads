I know i'm a little bit late on this but I still see a lot of little goblins still crying about freelook... here's my explanation and my opinion about it being removed..


1. Freelook gives you a visual advantage that you can't usually do in vanilla Minecraft.... ex: you're in bedwars and you're breaking a bed defense while freelooking around trying to find an enemy and you see him right on the bridge and turn around and kill him, without freelook, you can't do that.....

2. "Almost everyone uses freelook why not they just get it? it's literally free".... It's like saying "hacked clients are free, why not just use it"

3. What is the definition of an "unfair advantage" on the Hypixel network? unfair advantage is mostly things that gives you a sort of advantage that people on vanilla minecraft can't do... "what about armor hud or potion hud... its an advantage because they can see it without opening their inventory" that "advantage" doesn't affect other players, it's a cosmetic mod and Hypixel specifically said its allowed on the "allowed modification".... unfair advantages like reach, antikb, kill aura, bhop... affects other player experiences in Hypixel....


My opinions about it being removed: I don't really care but I use it a lot but I get it, its an unfair advantage and I respect it, it only took me 1 week to get used to f5 and im fine now
