When I saw SuchSpeed's video on this, I couldn't believe what I was seeing.

You may not remember this Era, or not even know it existed, but back in the day (2016-2017), there was this trend across Brazilian MC Servers and some other servers that obligated that when you joined, any staff could put you in a cage, and you were forced to show your whole PC to make sure you weren't hacking. And if you left the server, you were banned. If you had hacks only for anarchy servers, you would also be banned.

So, I was OUTRAGED, when I saw that in 2021, 5 YEARS LATER, there is this company, which we will call Avenge from now on, that makes Software, to detect hacks in People's PC to defeat hackers...

You may ask, what is the problem. Well, you see, that is the most ****ed up thing you can do.

That was NOT the old technique. That was a specialized software that would scan your PC for hacked clients, INCLUDING YOUR SEARCH HISTORY. This is a Major security flaw. Like, I made posts in the past about Hypixel Watchdog being installed locally on people's PCs, and people complain, yet this company think this is OK.

But, it gets worse. In order for it to work, you need to actually install their Software on the SUSpect's computer, using remote access. SuchSpeed showed us in his video, and I immediately asked myself: "Is this scripted?" Like, I kid you not, ALL the hackers he banned had AngDesk installed, used the same hack, same language(which by the way, was Italian. Italy is where the company is from) and same wallpaper. But that wasn't the worse part. Almost no one these days, unless they were Working from Home has AnyDesk installed.

And that brings me to my other point. Why would we be forced to install software that is pretty clearly Spyware, with NO TERMS OF SERVICE to be accepted. No one is going to install that.

And, this gets worse. It's not even free. If you want that defense for your network, you must pay. Now, there are really good AntiCheats that cost 20$ or less, so what is the price of this one? It is, for the Network Plan, 19$... PER MONTH... Don't get me wrong, you get more than you would get with Discord Nitro , but still, I find it an absolute scam.

So, conclusion time, this company is here, trying to revive an OLD AND SLOW method of banning hackers, and it's pricing it so high that it would be better to buy something else, like BAC.

This is honestly sad. Seeing potential that could be used for other things, wasted making this bull****. A big **** YOU AVENGE.

Also, F U to you too SuchSpeed. This video you made is honestly sad to look at. You make really good content and I enjoy it, but you went to far on this one, and I'm sorry, but I can't defend you here.

Tell me what you guys think
Best regards, Nyxw.
