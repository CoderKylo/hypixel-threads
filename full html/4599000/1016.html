The Dyna Translation Service

Hello everyone!
I've been noticing recently that a lot of people are having trouble understanding people since they either speak in a different language or have such horrible spelling it looks like a completely different language. That is why I have created the Dyna Translation Service. This project started a couple months ago and I'm finally ready to announce it.

Dyna Translation Service can be activated by pinging @aquaka in any forum post. It works 24/7 and on any forum subsection. Profile posts are currently offlimits and will not work. Announcement posts are also currently offlimit and will also not work.

Features of the Translation Service

Features of the Translation Service include free translation of up to 10 different languages, and the ability to translate idiot and kirbs language aswell as any bad spelling language. Additionally the Service comes with an UwU-ify feature.

Customer Reviews on the Dyna Translation Service

Customer reviews have all been very positive so far and I'm very happy with how far I've come with this project. If any problems arise make sure to start a forum convo with me so I can solve the problem.

Popular customer reviews:


As you can see, samq was down in the depths as no one could ever understand any words he said on the forums, but ever since he started using Dyna Translation Service he found peace as people were able to understand what he was saying!
