What is this?

This is essentially a contest where people can submit various threads that they have made to showcase their talent as a forumer when it comes to creating innovative threads. Once this thread has gone public users will have three weeks to create high quality threads that are meaningful to the community and benefit the forums / network in some way or form. There will be various threads that will receive honourable mentions but one thread will be chosen as the winner and that thread will receive a mod emerald.

Threads will be judged by me and various judges from different areas of the network and forums that are listed later on in this thread to ensure that this isn't biased in anyway.

One major disclaimer is that this isn't affiliated with the staff team at all, this is my sole decision to undertake this project.

Why is this happening?

There are various reasons why I want to make a contest for forum threads, the main two reasons are as follows. The first has to do with the entire theme of this contest, the staff reactions, specifically the mod emerald. Before game masters were a thing, there were an abundance of moderators who actively used the forums, and a much higher number of moderators who focused their efforts on forum moderation. Due to their being more moderators, the mod emerald which many forumers wanted to get was in a greater abundance back then, there were more moderators and so more people to give out this reaction. This is however no longer the case as there are significantly less game masters than moderators, because of this very few get given out now. In my eyes the mod emerald was something that should be given out to users who go above and beyond to deliver extraordinary content to the forums which benefit it in some way or form. I have only ever given out one mod emerald and I would love to give out more to make up for there being less staff to do so.

For anyone interested this is the thread I gave my one mod emerald to https://hypixel.net/threads/the-skyblock-forumer-quizlet-new-link.3860049/#post-27982438

The second reason for creating a contest like this is somewhat linked to the first reason, I want this thread to give people an additional reason to go above and beyond the standard level and deliver some exceptional content to the community. I want to reignite some people's hopes of still having a decent chance of receiving a staff reaction.

Depending on how good or bad the feedback of this thread is and the performance of this thread, there may be additional seasons of this championship in the future.
I however still wish for the mod emerald reaction to be pretty special so these events will be happening at most once every 3-4 months.

How to participate

In order to participate, all you have to do is reply to this thread with links to your threads with a brief summary of what they are and why you choose that sort of thread.

An example would be like this.
Thread: https://hypixel.net/threads/suggestion-csgo-overwatch-like-system-for-the-hypixel-network.3065122/
Summary: I introduced a concept for an atlas like system which later got added and made a significant impact on the cheater problem.
Why I made this thread: I understood a significant bottleneck in the way cheaters are handled by staff and provided a solution that would not only benefit the staff team but the community as a whole.

After three weeks (Please let me know if this should be changed), we will no longer accept any additional threads and will commence the judging phase.
The honourable mentions and winner will be announced in a separate thread.

Rules and regulations

There are a few rules that are set into motion to ensure that this contest is fair for all users.

- Each user can only submit threads that they have posted.
- Each user can only submit a maximum of three threads.
- No copying other users threads.
- Please avoid controversial topics, this is meant to create a positive impact.
- Please submit all three threads in a single message on this thread, edit your message if you want to add more.
- Please don't submit troll threads that have no real meaning.
- Please ensure that all content you create and submit conforms to the official rules at https://hypixel.net/rules.
- The threads you submit can only be threads created after this threads creation to ensure a fair contest.
- Threads will not be counted after the time limit no matter the circumstances.
- Don't attempt to bribe the judges.
- You have until the 11th of November 16:00 GMT / 12:00 EST.

Judges

As stated in this thread already, there will be judges to assist me in reviewing all the thread submissions to ensure that my single opinion doesn't cloud my view on things, they are here to make the judgements more sophisticated and diverse to prevent a biased perspective. I may look for more judges in future seasons. These judges cannot participate themselves.

- @RRBuilder
- @Tetracade
- @_Area52_
- @Aparting
- @CaptainSonic
- @Huger

Closing statement

I hope this is the start of a great community interaction that inspires the creation of some creative and meaningful content that benefits the community and the forums.

Feel free to send feedback on this if you have any suggestions or improvements that can be made.

Good luck to all the participants!

UPDATE:

The mod emerald has been removed.

There is now a new reaction that will be counted separately to the mod emerald. It has the same pixel art but it called "GMerald".
