1. Enderman had used to have green eyes before they were added.
2. Netherite armor used to be crafted by putting a diamond tool/armor next to a netherite ingot in the crafting recipe.
3. There are 3 types of air blocks: air, void air, and cave air
4. The Mossy cobblestone found in Giant Tree Taiga Biomes is actually a structure, called the "Forest Rock".
5. Hunger regeneration goes faster in Java Edition than Bedrock Edition.
