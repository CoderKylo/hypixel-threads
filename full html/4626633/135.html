All mods are just straight up BAD.
I can be cool with you if you put a 3 inch lift kit on your Tahoe or something, but I will never be cool with you if you put a 6 inch lift kit on, these new wheels with the ugliest patterns I've ever seen in my entire life, and add stickers to your back window.
It just ruins the truck.
The most common offenders are the Jeep people, they ruin their vehicles all day every day cause they think they're so cool and hip. They aren't. Why not just leave your stock wheels on and only add like a 3 inch lift? Why ruin your truck and then think you're the king of the road? You clearly aren't.
The middle aged men just LOVE doing this and I will never understand why. Dont even get me started on the normal vehicles. They add like these 48575 foot wings on the back of their car even though all that down force isn't needed, You're not going to fly away in a Honda Civic middle aged man.
Then they add all these bodykits. STOP RUINING YOUR CARS WITH THESE. They are uglier than anything I've seen in my sort of near 2 decades of life. Then they add these turbos and cut their exhaust to the point where there isn't even one anymore just to make their car sound better. Turbos are unnecessary in almost every circumstance. The only time I would understand having a turbo is during a race.
Then, they camber their wheels to the point where they're looking like this is the Delorean or something.

STOP. ALL I ASK OF YOU MIDDLE AGED MEN IS TO STOP.

Thanks for coming to my rant, share your opinion if you'd like.
