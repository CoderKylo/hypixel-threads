As the Minecon live event ended, the mob vote got it's result in the form of allay. First I tell you about all the mobs in the mob vote.

Glare- This mob will give a grumpy look in dark places as it hates darkness. It's work is to alert players about dark places. Pretty useless mob in my opinion and was the first mob to get eliminated from the event.

Copper Golem- These are the smaller variants of iron golem which can be crafted using crafting. It would randomly press any copper button near it. It is good for redstone mechanics at high level. It fought with the allay till the end but allay won. I was with copper golem.

Allay- The winner of mob vote 2021, allay is music lover mob which randomly picks items from ground and collect same item from the ground nearby. Note it that it does not duplicate the items. It just collect it from ground. Don't give it diamonds or netherite in hope of getting more dias and netherites.

Now the thing is that the winning of allay doesn't make sense as allay isn't that useful. It is clearly better than glare but I think that copper golem was the most deserving mob as it would give copper a good use(I found a lot of copper in my world which seems useless to me). Tell me which mob should have won the mob vote.
