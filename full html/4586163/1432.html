What is on your bucket list
(A Bucket list is an list of things you want to get done/do, only including irl stuff for your first one)

Here is mine
See a lightning bolt
See a lightning bolt hit some object- think it would be cool
Visit an active volcano- volcanoes always gave me awe as a kid
See actual lava- I want to throw a berry into lava (from a distance ofc)
Visit Japan(I just want to see what their cities look like)

Go to a convention
(Never been to one)
Visit Taiwan/Hong Kong
(My parents birthplaces
Successfully grow a bamboo plant without it dying for 3 years(2/3 years)(just into it and to remind myself I can keep something alive)
Spot a wild wolf in the wild(wolves were my favorite animal for the past 4 years)
Spot a wild deer or moose in the wild(would be cooler if the wolf killed the deer)
Visit a place covered in snow
(Been to snowy places but hasn’t been snowing)


Now the second one is for gaming
Get mvp++ when I’m tired of Green rank(which is not yet)
Get fishing 26 on sb(for rod)
Beat the game legit in single player minecraft(always fail at fortress)
Beat Three Goblets (last level involves killing side men and it’s so Grindy)
Get a score of 130 on Slope the game(high score 98, just so I can wipe the look on the sophomore face with his 108 score)
Buy and try subnautica(looked like a cool game would prob only pick it up once tho due to fear lol)
Breed a blue axolotl in minecraft(something I wanted to do)
Get all banner patterns legit in minecraft (only thing missing is creeper pattern)

Get a legendary weapon by crafting in Warlords(so I can finally get dmg)

So these are mine,
