If you enjoy this please let me know, and I can continue this further and put more effort into this. I'll probably continue it anyway but I'll most likely get bored if no one's interested. Skywars is a fast paced competitive minecraft gamemode that pits players head to head in a variety of different locatio-
BORING! SHUT UP NERD!

The non closes YouTube and opens minecraft. He aggressively punches the derpy skywars gatekeeper who sluggishly asks while whether he would like to play insane or normal. The non thinks carefully. He had read that insane mode spawn you with better loot, leading to faster more 'insane' gameplay as the name suggests. Then suddenly, right before his eyes he found himself in a floating glass cage, suspended in the sky. The non looked around, astonished. There were 11 other players, just like him, in glass cages, just like him. And in an instant, the ticking noise that he hadn't even realised was there stopped., and everyone was plummeting downwards, just like him.

Once again, I hope you enjoyed this, cos I had fun writing it and yeah, if you thought it was semi-decent and your brain cells may just be able to cope with a bit more, let me know!
