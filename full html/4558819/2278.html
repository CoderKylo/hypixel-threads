I just realised, I haven't given this thread an actual response yet
(I spent some time grafting a detailed one but my Internet connection butchured it so let me try to rewrite it again in short)
-The enemy attacks are interesting and challenging to avoid but also simple, easy t digest and requires skill much more than pure RNG which I find great, gameplay-wise
-I wish the game was more open-worlded than "take this path to talk to a random NPC then get back to the main story" because I often feel railroaded. I understand a large yet hogh-quality world would be especially hard to build, though
-The plot is amazing and the characters and their interactions are both realistic and hilarious. Especially The Queen With Her Throwing Glass. And how Berdy simps for her
-Berdly has no right having such a banger for a theme, but then again Toby did outdo himself for the entire OST so yeah
Those were my thoughts! Maybe I should put this on Steam lol
