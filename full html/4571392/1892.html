ShakISMETHEBEST is a casual ****poster like every average hypixel forums enjoyer, but in recent month, they started to build some kind of their own story or should I say, a thread universe. If you don't know who this person is (which is like 50% chance) I will look deep into this hellhole of ****posts to try to understnad this universe. It's even better than star wars or avengers, bear with me. I just want to say that I'm not hating on this person, I don't want to public shame them, I just want to see how did this not so average ****posting story began. And also cuz im bored and this megathread by Shak was the first thing I saw here today.
CHAPTER 1 : Tlauncher player
Shak's epic journey through countries, hospitals and probably even time and space started on January the 8th 2021. Her first thread was "how to join hypixel servers". She sad she was playing on Tlauncher. Some forumers helped her, some laughed at her. One forumer though, one forumer replied supportively to this thread and supported her ever since. But they are just a side character in this drama anime. The toxicity is probably the reason this tragic story began. After this thread, some of her next threads looked like a troll. Adding a hinduism mode in BedWars (she is Indian btw) , adding a hinduism mode to SkyBlock and stuff like this. She was also a big anti-toxic warrior. Sometimes she just posted "pls stop being toxic" and I fully respect that. She wanted to really get minecraft for free and stop hackers. Now, this doesn't sound so bad, but the greates turn of events is still far away.
CHAPTER 2 : Making history
This chapter starts with new and reformed Shak. On May 30th 2021 she made a thread that changed her forums career. She said she will stop doing annoying threads and instead, she'll make history... and informative threads. And history, she did. Probably, idk. After that there were still some threads supporting cracked mc and how she bought mc but still won't play on premium servers which I fully respect. But on September 11th 2021 she made a thread saying ta´hat her aunt died from cancer. I don't want to go deep into this specific thread nor make fun of it because I really do think her aunt died because there can't be a normal human being who would fake her aunt's death for clout right? Her next thread also was that she started to suffer from type 1 diabetes but don't worry, she'll forget about this a week later I think. She'll also go to USA for a day because of the aunt but again, I don't want to say anything about it.
CHAPTER 3 : Roadtrip around the world
This chapter turned on a dark turn. On September 18th, her school was cancelled because 4 of her teachers died from covid. But actually after that, she said they all died of lung cancer, which was very interesting turn of events. She also said that she wants to kill herself But don't worry, her dad gave her the option to choose what place to travel to with her 2 best teachers because of the 4 teachers that died. I don't know why exactly with 2 teachers and in the middle of September but alright. Day later she was already in Tokyo which was great. And hey, she even said she has school there. On Thursday (September 23rd) She's going from Tokyo to Rome as her next trip with her Dad, 2 teachers and.... a Japanese friend? The story is getting better, they kidnapped a Japanese boy or girl, awesome. And now the backstory. Finally, we got backstory. She said that she lived in USA before they moved to India when she was 6. She's describing a scary story about a man that sneaked to their house and killed himself when her parents called 911. This is probably why she wants to kill herself. And now, the plottwist. Yesterday (on September 26th) Her sister Mina said that Shak killed herself.... This was very unexpected and sad. But stop the tears ladies and gentlemen. Mina said that Shak isn't dead yet and she'll probably recover in 4 hours. But wait, how did Shak's sister knew Shak killed herself, isn't Shak in Rome??? ALSO Shak's sister said that she suffered from brain damage and cancer... No mentions of diabetes at all. Shak is probably not a human. She got a cancer... In the same day she tried to kill herself AND THEN SHE RECOVERED IN JUST 6 HOURS. Shak is probably the strongest main character. Even better than Goku or Saitama. The next thread was the thread where everyone realized this story was a fake.After 6 hours of no threads she said she is alive again. (after 6 hours) and said that she is in coma while typing a thread.... After 4 hours from the last thread, she made a thread about being fully recovered and that shecan finally go on her next trip because she is no longer banned from travelling. She got probably banned because of the kidnapping. Even though she said she'll be going on 2 trips cuz of the 4 teachers, she's going to Mexico City and Vienna next. And now we're waiting for another thread.
Summary​
As we're waiting for another thread or should I say another manga chapter I'll review it. My final rating is 2/10. Sometimes was the story emotional but unfortunately, there are too many plotholes. I hope this story isn't real. I hope it's just a big ****post. Making ****post story about trying to ************* is not funny, but I don't want to insult her. She's probably just bored and making stories up. I also did that when I was younger, but I uploaded it on Wattpad lol. If this thread is insulting to anyone, I'll delete it. Goodbye.
