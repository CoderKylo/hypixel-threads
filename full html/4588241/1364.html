Common hacks:
Killaura - Locks camera onto you and seems to click really, really fast. Sometimes auraers quickly switch between 2 targets.
Bhop (bunny hop) - Goes too fast, has movement too janky for a speed potion. A similar hack lets you sprint in all directions.
Reach - Hits you from farther than is typically possible.
Fly - fly
AntiKB (knockback) - Doesn't take proper knockback.
Jesus - Walks or sprints on water.
Scaffold - Head snaps back and forth while placing blocks.
There's several other hacks that allow you to eat and attack, or bot certain games, but those are less common.

Oh god, here we go. No I dont hack, never will, but I play pit, and anyone who plays pit, knows this crap. Ok let’s go.
Reach: Quite literally reach
Scaffold: Places block below you as you walk over empty spaces
Autoclicker: Clicks for you
Aimassist/Aimbot: Moves your crosshair with the nearest player
Bhop: Allows you to move in unnatural ways
Timer: Client side tick speed increase
Flight: Well …. It’s flying
Chams/ESP/Nametags: All let you see thing you aren’t supposed too (People through walls, chests through walls, what armour and health people are at)
Kilaura: Automatically hits people within your chosen range, and includes features like hitting through walls, autoblocking, etc.
Velocity: Reduced your knockback taken

And yeah, there’s a lot more, but most of them are less common. If I forgot something obvious please tell me!
