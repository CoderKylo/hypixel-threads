I just bought G610 Orion, and I want to share my first impressions on it.

So, G610 Orion is a full-sized mechnical keyboard, with some custom keys as built-in. The custom keys are: Game Mode toggle key, keyboard brightness key, mute key, 4 keys for playing videos, and a wheel for volume. It also comes with indicators to show whether or not number lock is on, caps lock is on, or scroll lock is on.

The pluses​

The volume wheel is one of the nicest qol thing I've ever seen on a keyboard. It can adjust the audio volume with just a scroll, without ever needing to press window key. The "Video" keys work on Youtube, which is all I could ask for. It also feels nice to type and play games with this keyboard. You can also adjust the brightness of your keyboard with just a press of a button. I also was surprised to find out that there are a lot of lighting thing on the keyboard. But I'm just gonna use "Echo Press".

The minuses​

First things first, this keyboard costs a lot. By that I mean more than 100$. Also the indicator lights for num lock, caps lock, and scroll lock of my g610 doesn't work for some reason, it could just be my problem, but I thought it's worth mentioning nontheless. Earlier in the thread I mentioned that there is a button to toggle 'game mode', it took me like 25 minutes to find it. The icon on the button looks like a 'lock' button, instead of like game controller button. I'm a little upset about that.

A few things to note​

I was really surprised to find out that this keyboard is the loudest keyboard I've ever used. Even with ear phones with a music on, I could clearly hear myself mashing buttons on my keyboard. I did some googling and it turns out that it's just because it's a mechnical keyboard. I guess it's good for K&M sounds type of videos. The keyboard I got is actually a G610 Orion Blue, it registers on Ghub(it actually says "G610" instead of "G610 Orion" on ghub), so I think it's official thing. But as of writing this thread you cannot find "G610 Orion" on official Logitech G website for whatever reason. "Then how did you get it?" you may ask, I actually just bought it from an offline market for around 100$.

I got gifted a razer keyboard and I wasn't on planning to use it until my old keyboard started to break, so I started using the one I got gifted, at first I didn't really like it (I sound spoiled) because of how small and LOUD it was. But some days later, I actually really liked the size. My old keyboard covered like half of the desk, so this smaller keyboard was great, but the only issue I had after a few months of using it was how loud it was. My parents actually bought me the same old keyboard because they didn't like the sound, but I kept using the razer one because I liked it.

Then it broke, so I started using the large, less quiet keyboard. I still miss the razer one though, it saves a lot of room for me.
