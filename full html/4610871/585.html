Hello, I was planning on making this thread a few days ago but I keep forgetting each time. As you are probably aware, cecer tends to do music parties in the hypixel discord and host them in housing and then play mini games after, this is around every 3-4 months but there isn't really a proper schedule as that's not cecer job. But every time one is announced, I usually make a thread about it to spread the news as I know there are people who will likely be interested in going to this event. This event will be halloween themed I think, so it might have halloween music if that's the case. If you want to know more information, just head over to the hypixel discord and scroll down until you see this:

Head over to miscellaneous and you'll find all the details! Unfortunately there is no exact time when it'll be starting on october 30th, but it'll likely be around 12 pm est to 3 pm est. These events tend to last 7-8 hours long I believe, so you don't have to be there for the entire event, but I'll definitely be there for the entire event that's for sure, one of my favorite things about hypixel is being in the community and you can be sure I'll be in this lol. Hopefully some of you guys can make it, but if not then that's fine.

Also happy spooky month.

EDIT: Since the thread is kinda disorganized, I'll post all the important information you need to know in one spot
Where: In the hypixel discord, you can also visit cecer's housing if you are able to be on the server
When: October 30th, around 12 pm est to 3 pm est likely. However the exact time when the event will happen in october 30th is not known yet.
How long: They tend to last around 6-8 hours, so expect quite a bit of fun!
