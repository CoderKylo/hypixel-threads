so this got me thinking, can you kill a god,
btw this quote was a joke, taken from https://hypixel.net/threads/give-me-any-phrase-part-2-d.4630051/#post-33416209
Explanation
so anyways, how does one go about killing a god?
its simple, gods exist on belief, according to most religions, this means that if they are not widely believed in they get a low weaker, and occasionally die. This excludes religions that worship "God" or Allah, which believe that the divine creator of all things is a deity that exists above us, and completely separate to us, it is not a being, it is a superior concept that supersedes our existence, which means we would have no reason to kill it, as it is above conflict and mortal anger. I dont understand Hinduism and wont mention it, as i have not learned about it at all.
Norse
So the original question, how to kill a god, well, according to the norse mythos, all beings will die, they are more like strong humans who live for a long time, rather than gods, a sufficient weapon such as thors hammer would do the job, or even an enchanted mistletoe, according to some myths
Greek/Roman/Other religions that sprouted from the olympians as they follow the same rules
In greek mythos, gods can fade, not die, but its the same thing, based on the power of belief, if you do not believe in gods, the less effect or control they have over you, considering how weak they would be due to a lack of belief, you could use the weapons of the gods, such as zueses bolts, or any other godly weapon, supposedly, the god pan (the god of nature and natural places) died, due to humans pillaging his natural realm, weakening him to the point of mortality, where he dies.
Eygpt
Egyptians have Osiris, who dies, and becomes an undead god, just kill the host and the god gets weakened to the point of which they can no longer kill you, they will eventually regenerate and be fine in the long run
Celts
i havnt read up on the celts, so ill just go on what little ik, the giants and gods are like the norse giants and gods, they can die, they fight wars, get their heads chopped off, and bing bong life is gone, pretty simple, though i dont know if you need a sufficient weapon such as the sword of Cuchulain, which was blessed by the gods, or if i could pick up a machete and charge into battle against these powerful forces of nature. From what little research ive done, i honestly cant tell if celts and germanicus are the same, so ima pretend they are.
Ight im too lazy to do any more, if you want me to do a special religion, i will, just ping me
so the answer?
it depends on what god you want to kill, but in general yes, you can, you can almost always incapacitate them, and slow them/push them back in order to live out your life, if you are skilled enough, you can even escape death itself, depending on the religiong

The entire plot of Morrowind is that you kill a god, then proceed to kill two more gods because the first god you killed told you so and also you are the reincarnation of the guy that got killed by the second two gods.

So, to kill a god. First, find their anchor. For example: Dagoth Ur and the Tribunal uses the Heart of Lorkhan as their main source of energy. Destroy it, or cut off access to it. Then kill them.

If they are an Aedra, Daedra, or not once a mortal, simply kill all of their worshipers. The power of a god depends on their worshipers, which is why the glorious Aldmeri Dominion and the wise Thalmor banned the worship of the false god Tiber Septim to weaken the powerless Empire.

Third, use other gods to "kill" their colleagues. The Daedra Lords feared Jyggalag, so they forced him into Sheogorath, the one thing he hated most. Jyggalag then forgot who he was and became Sheogorath.
