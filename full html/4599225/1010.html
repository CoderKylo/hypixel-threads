What are the best gaming mice?
the best mice are the ones that get really high cps and some mice can double click which means when you click once,it registers 2 clicks.
To double click, you have don't really need to have a good mouse. sometimes mice that are older can double click better than new ones.
Most new mice have double click prevention witch prevents you from double clicking. that is bad. we want cps. Some mice that can double click are the glorious model, g Logitech mice, razer mice (only the old ones), and the Redragon m711. But a few special kinds of mice can quadruple click. there are only 2 mice that can quadruple click, the bloody a60, and the HyperX Pulsefire Haste (I have this mouse). you may ask, "Butt how do ve double click eh?" its simple. you have to slap your button with your finger on the side of the button. Theres a chance that your mouse has double click prevention so you have to buy another one. But what Is the best gaming mice.

What are the best gaming mice?
The best mice is the HyperX Pulsefire Haste
HyperX Pulsefire Haste:
Butterfly clicking: 16 cps
Jitter clicking 15 cps
Drag clicking: 24 cps
and since it can quadruple click, this makes godbridging insanely easy.


But here are the best mice for every clicking method.
Butterfly clicking: G305 / Glorious model o
Dragclicking: Bloody a60 / Glorious model o
Jitterclicking: Glorious model o


Tier list:
HyperX Pulsefire Haste - Best overall
HyperX Pulsefire Haste - Best for quadruple clicking.
Bloody A60 - Best for drag clicking
G305 - Best for butterfly clicking
Redragon M711 - Great budget mouse
Glorious Model O - Amazing if you want to get good at pvp
Razer naga pro - Most YouTubers use this.

So the best mice is the HyperX Pulsefire Haste...
