You can learn different combo methods such as s-tapping, w-tapping, and straffing (is this how you spell it?). I've seen people like iusehunzi (you can search up his yt) play sumo and combo people with 1 cps by w-tapping or s-tapping. In ordder to use these two methods, you need to press the s key every 2 seconds (i think) while sprinting, and let go of the w key every 2 seconds (again i think) while sprinting. If those two methods are too hard for you, then I would suggest straffing. In order to straffe, you need to have at least 12 cps (this is what i have seen). You move side to side depending on which way the player's head is facing. I know RiceFarmer11 has a youtube tutorial on all of these combo methods. I hope this helped, and good luck!

it's strafing and you can combo ppl with 5-6 cps (altho higher cps does help and on servers with as much lag as hypixel, comboing seems impossible with low cps after you've gotten used to high cps)

higher ping means that your hitreg is significantly worse than most ppl's but it also means that you take 0 kb (in most cases) and get ridiculously far double/triple hits when blockhit spamming so use it to your advantage

because of your 0 kb, you can also combo pretty easily by w/s tapping and strafing because after you start a combo, you get insaane reach on the server's side whereas on your screen, you're comboing them from a reasonable distance (less than 3 blocks), but it goes both ways and if someone starts a combo on you, they look like they're using insane reach on your screen whereas that's just your ping so at that point, you want to pray that you take 0 kb and get a counter hit or that they mess up the combo
