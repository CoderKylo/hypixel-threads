This is only my OPINION, so you may not entirely agree with me. I’m just stating what I think.


Glare

This mob is decent but also kind of useless since torches exist. It alerts you when it’s dark enough for mobs to spawn. When it’s dark in a cave you already know that there will be mobs spawning in anyways, and at night of course. But I guess this is pretty good if you’re building and you want to know which places you should put torches.

Allay

This is pretty good for farms, hoppers would be pretty much useless when this is added into this game. Give it one item and put it in the mob farm and it sorts the things out for you and give the ones you need. Good if you don’t want to waste space. If you think it duplicates items, you’re wrong. It only picks up items that are dropped on the ground in loaded chunks. So if you give it a piece of cobblestone it will pick up any nearby pieces that are on the ground. Good for farms and sorting items.

Copper Golem

Now this gives copper more use. It could be really good for redstone since it likes to press copper buttons. If you wanna make a farm or redstone machine that involves pressing buttons, this is the mob for you. More advanced redstone creations will be built. Traps will evolve. Mumbo Jumbo, you better get ready for this!

Overall, the Copper Golem is the most useful in my eyes, more complex farms and redstone creations will be made. Traps could be useful with this too.
