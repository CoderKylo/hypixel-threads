ah yes another bedwars related thread







































if you're new to mechanical keyboards I'd recommend getting blues (loud and tactile), browns (quieter but not silent and tactileish), or reds (quieter but not silent and linear - little to no tactility)

there's no best switch for minecraft or gaming in general or anything, it's all up to preference

if your parents (and whoever lives around you) doesn't mind the noise and you like it, blues are a great starting option

if you think that your keyboard being loud could be a problem then go for browns or reds (browns being more tactile than reds)

if you already have experience with mechanical keyboards, use what switch you like best (feels/sounds the most comfortable)
