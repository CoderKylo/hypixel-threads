Ex-hacker can answer. They hack for a variety of reasons. To look cool, to get pleasure out of having an advantage that is almost godlike, to just lay waste to a bunch of stuff. Some do it for fun on anarchy servers, others have a mission to destroy some servers based on personal preferences or reasons (ie the server is P2W). I did it because I was miserable and it was fun to kill people in full diamond with my fists and I liked watching fire. Some hack just for automation, like with Baritone, the autominer. I never hacked on competitive servers. Usually it would be anarchy or servers without an anticheat. You find out some funny things like mute bypasses and so on, but I digress. In general, if not sanctioned by a server, it is a garbage thing to do. I don't stand by my past actions, except in a few cases where the staff were corrupt and I did it as a form of protest. It's been a while since I used that profile, and it probably will be a long time before I use it again, if at all. 2B2T is cool but the queue is forever.

Some people do it to inflate their self worth, others do it for fun, others do it because they are miserable and just like the destruction, and still others do it because they have a legitimate reason to (to force change on servers that violate the EULA or are horrifically toxic, like that Team Avolition video where they took out the staff member's house who was talking to underage players). As for Hypixel hackers, I have no clue.
