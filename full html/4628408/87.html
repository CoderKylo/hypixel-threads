This has several major flaws that all stem from the same idea so let me explain
visjava said:
if u have the right mouse
Click to expand...
This right here is what eliminates it as being a skill, what equipment you have should not determine whether you are good or bad at something, it should only limit your capabilities to become your best. Giving a bad tennis racket to a professional tennis player will not make him lose all their skills, he will just be at a disadvantage. However using a mouse that can't be drag clicked on is like giving a pro tennis player a ping pong racket and telling them to play against someone with a tennis racket. They are simply not on the same league and the pro tennis player with the ping pong racket would be lucky to land a point.
visjava said:
but using drag clicking in game, for things like bridging and pvp are about skill
Click to expand...
referring to my previous point, a drag clicking mouse is a piece of equipment that enables you to do extra things. The actual skills in that sentence of yours are bridging and pvp as you don't need top of the line products to be good. Using top of the line products can instead enhance your ability which is what drag clicking mice are, they are products that are built to enhance the amount of cps you get. Drag clicking on them is just a feature that they support.

visjava said:
also drag clicking is allowed on hypixel, so is having low debounce time.
Click to expand...
this is also not entirely true, changing debounce times is suggested against doing so and could be considered a cheat as it is 3rd party software that alters your mouse behavior in order to gain a cps advantage. You can also refer to this post where it is said that it's advised against doing so:
https://hypixel.net/threads/how-to-lower-debounce-time-on-a-razer-mouse.4563338/#post-32944549

As for drag clicking being allowed, it's not allowed but not disallowed, it sits on the same level as mods where each and everyone is use at your own risk. Drag clicking is use at your own risk because it has the potential to flag watchdog for autoclicking and if you do get banned for it, you have very little ground to stand on and try to prove your point.

Drag clicking mice provide too much overlap for equipment advantages compared to skill that imo drag clicking on these mice is not a skill but instead a feature of the mouse that enables you to do such things.

An example is like hitting a tennis ball very fast. If you have a super light racket you can swing it faster and thus generate a greater speed on the ball compared to say using a heavier one. Or even depending on how tight the strings are on the racket you can get more speed. But the actual skill behind this is landing the ball in the court. Most people can swing a racket quite hard and generate some speed on the ball but hitting the ball in the court is the hard part that requires skill.

Same with drag clicking, drag clicking isn't too hard on it's own, drag your finger and that's mostly it, it's actually the pvp and god bridging that is the skill, you simply use drag clicking to generate clicks, the way you do it leverages the ability that the mouse provides and thus enhances your playstyle to achieve such things much easier. However you can also be good at pvp without drag clicking, for example @StevenR8 , really good bedwars player and doesn't drag click and I think plays on an office mouse. He doesn't need a drag clicking mouse because he is actually skilled, using a drag clicking mouse might enhance his overall game though as it may provide extra cps that was originally unattainable.

Hopefully this helps show my point and why something like drag clicking is not really a skill.
