It has been done. I have been on this website for exactly 1 year. I don’t know how I feel.

First time on forums

I had officially started using forums on November 4th, a day after my birthday. I guess I had typed “hy” in my search bar and noticed I had a Hypixel forums account. Curious, I’m assuming I clicked and scrolled through. Things looked so messy. I was lost. But then I found the Off Topic section which had ultimately changed my entire experience on forums.

I was into skyblock at that time, but had not noticed the skyblock section. And I’m glad I had not noticed it at that moment.

But before I did anything, I wanted to mess with my account settings a bit since I didn’t like the google letter pfp. I looked up “walrus” on google and found one that was pixelated in the form of a minecraft skull.

Anyhow, I returned to the Off Topic section and clicked on Forum Games. The first thread I saw was “Last to Post Wins [Season 3].” Oh that sparks memories. I replied with something dumb like “hello” and instantly got “out newbied” by @Ewianking who, at the time, was the king of LTP. I also met people like @burnt_out_ashes and @MeAmFoxBoi, who actively changed their pfp based on their mood. I won’t forget how cute some of those foxes were.

There was also an election going on at around the 10th or 11th of November, which I made an alt for. I currently have that alt account with no trace to its past, I think. Anyways, I think someone along the name of polar something was running against foxboi.

November - December 2020

My catchphrase during those few months was “yeet” and I have said it in multiple threads over 500 times at least. I forget how immature I was…

I also met some people like sykese who was a Well-known member since there was no dedicated+. I mean, there was the reactions used for years, no reaction bar or guild info under the username.

I was also known as a ****poster due to some postfarming and dumb threads. This, however, changed later on into 2021.

January - The spark of something new

January. It was the month I started the daily dose series. AKA, one of the longest running series on forums. I started it out of boredom. It turned out to be something I was passionate about and never let go of.

I was also under the alias of Wafflress which had begun when someone had made a game about waffles.

February - May

During this time, forums was changing. I was also changing as a person socially. I started to have friends on forums. People that I smiled about when I saw them.

Back to me changing. I was starting to understand how big of a ****ing ****poster I was so I changed that. My activity decreased by like 1000%. I made like 3-4 posts per day including the ddorf series. The activity only decreased from March until this moment where I now keep a usual 9 or so posts per day, which slowly add to my message count.

June - September

It really is just the same cycle on forums.

However, in July, I did change my pfp from the usual walrus to anime one, which was something I had wanted to do ever since February, but just couldn’t do. It was around the time I was having several existential crises which probably was the best time to do it.

And I guess there was also Forumer Showdown, which I proudly got 2nd place in after taking a loss to Centranos. I don’t understand how I got second though. I’m not even that relevant. It may have been since I was somewhat of an underdog + the support of my daily dose series. But other than that, I don’t know.

October - Now

It has been a cycle. A cycle that you wouldn’t mind leaving, but don’t want to leave. It’s a weird cycle.

Do I enjoy forums?

I mean, I guess I do, but it isn’t something I smile about anymore. It’s just a part of my life. Not like infringing on my life or anything, but y’know, it’s just a part of it.

Do I regret joining?

Absolutely; however, it may be the reason why I am not a jerk in real life. I feel like forums have shaped that part of me that was just a jerk. But that could just be me and forums happened to be there during that time.

Do I have any goals on forums?

No. I’m just here to slowly build my army for when I run for ruler of the universe.

Jokes aside, I’m really here for the community. Off Topic may have been in ruins if it wasn’t for me, who knows (i highly doubt it). But I don’t even play minecraft anymore so my presence on this minecraft forum is pointless. I might as well be on twitter.

But, I prefer it here.

i’m glad i’m in this timeline. thank you guys, cheers

It has been done. I have been on this website for exactly 1 year. I don’t know how I feel.

First time on forums

I had officially started using forums on November 4th, a day after my birthday. I guess I had typed “hy” in my search bar and noticed I had a Hypixel forums account. Curious, I’m assuming I clicked and scrolled through. Things looked so messy. I was lost. But then I found the Off Topic section which had ultimately changed my entire experience on forums.

I was into skyblock at that time, but had not noticed the skyblock section. And I’m glad I had not noticed it at that moment.

But before I did anything, I wanted to mess with my account settings a bit since I didn’t like the google letter pfp. I looked up “walrus” on google and found one that was pixelated in the form of a minecraft skull.

Anyhow, I returned to the Off Topic section and clicked on Forum Games. The first thread I saw was “Last to Post Wins [Season 3].” Oh that sparks memories. I replied with something dumb like “hello” and instantly got “out newbied” by @Ewianking who, at the time, was the king of LTP. I also met people like @burnt_out_ashes and @MeAmFoxBoi, who actively changed their pfp based on their mood. I won’t forget how cute some of those foxes were.

There was also an election going on at around the 10th or 11th of November, which I made an alt for. I currently have that alt account with no trace to its past, I think. Anyways, I think someone along the name of polar something was running against foxboi.

November - December 2020

My catchphrase during those few months was “yeet” and I have said it in multiple threads over 500 times at least. I forget how immature I was…

I also met some people like sykese who was a Well-known member since there was no dedicated+. I mean, there was the reactions used for years, no reaction bar or guild info under the username.

I was also known as a ****poster due to some postfarming and dumb threads. This, however, changed later on into 2021.

January - The spark of something new

January. It was the month I started the daily dose series. AKA, one of the longest running series on forums. I started it out of boredom. It turned out to be something I was passionate about and never let go of.

I was also under the alias of Wafflress which had begun when someone had made a game about waffles.

February - May

During this time, forums was changing. I was also changing as a person socially. I started to have friends on forums. People that I smiled about when I saw them.

Back to me changing. I was starting to understand how big of a ****ing ****poster I was so I changed that. My activity decreased by like 1000%. I made like 3-4 posts per day including the ddorf series. The activity only decreased from March until this moment where I now keep a usual 9 or so posts per day, which slowly add to my message count.

June - September

It really is just the same cycle on forums.

However, in July, I did change my pfp from the usual walrus to anime one, which was something I had wanted to do ever since February, but just couldn’t do. It was around the time I was having several existential crises which probably was the best time to do it.

And I guess there was also Forumer Showdown, which I proudly got 2nd place in after taking a loss to Centranos. I don’t understand how I got second though. I’m not even that relevant. It may have been since I was somewhat of an underdog + the support of my daily dose series. But other than that, I don’t know.

October - Now

It has been a cycle. A cycle that you wouldn’t mind leaving, but don’t want to leave. It’s a weird cycle.

Do I enjoy forums?

I mean, I guess I do, but it isn’t something I smile about anymore. It’s just a part of my life. Not like infringing on my life or anything, but y’know, it’s just a part of it.

Do I regret joining?

Absolutely; however, it may be the reason why I am not a jerk in real life. I feel like forums have shaped that part of me that was just a jerk. But that could just be me and forums happened to be there during that time.

Do I have any goals on forums?

No. I’m just here to slowly build my army for when I run for ruler of the universe.

Jokes aside, I’m really here for the community. Off Topic may have been in ruins if it wasn’t for me, who knows (i highly doubt it). But I don’t even play minecraft anymore so my presence on this minecraft forum is pointless. I might as well be on twitter.

But, I prefer it here.

i’m glad i’m in this timeline. thank you guys, cheers
