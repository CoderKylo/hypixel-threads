We've finally made it to 100 lists! Woo!

I might get back into fact list making around November, but for now I'm going to be taking a break.
I'm not sure why this series never got much attention. Whenever I make an effort thread, it always seems like nobody actually clicks on it. However, it's still been quite an exciting and educative ride nonetheless. I would like to thank all of the regular readers of this series, and even just anyone who took the time out of their day to intrigue their curiosities and learn something random but hopefully interesting. Without further ado, let the facts begin.

1. Over 2,000 facts have been written over the course of this series

2. Random Facts Video Games Edition took the longest to write- 4 weeks in total.

3. The longest list in terms of content is Random Facts USA Part 2 Edition with 250 facts, 5 for each state.

4. I got the idea for this series from @S2eforme's fact list: https://hypixel.net/threads/random-facts-week-1.2302826/

5. The fact list with the most positive ratings is the very first one, with 9 positive ratings total.

6. 38 of the lists in this series have featured two topics.

7. The most viewed fact list is Random Facts USA Edition with 548 views.

8. This series was started on September 9th, 2019. It's been over two years since the first list.

9. 8 lists have been from requests. @Ewianking submitted the most requests with a total of 3.

10. This series is sponsored by Raid: Shadow Legends.
