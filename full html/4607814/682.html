The difference is that, compared to the other's initial products, battery life sucked, while Apple managed to keep and expand battery life and crush the competition with it's implementation.

What I'm trying to say here is that Hypixel can't be waiting for tech to become perfect for years. Somethings like Packet Transfering which affects the whole network are understandable, but not being able to implement that because of a minor thing, I don't think would be the best idea, specially if players really wanted.

That is my take on it. And if I was Simon and the players really wanted, I would at least try and I would make a post talking about it and how the experiences went when testing the features.
