hello

1. asking for a pencil

science class started and as usual, i didn’t have a pencil. so while i was looking everywhere in my backpack, the person i kind of like hands me one of their pencils. i kind of stood there like “wut” but realized what happened so i said “thank you.” i didn’t even ask for a pencil. they literally just knew from seeing what i was doing.

after the notes we were writing down, i gave the pencil back to them and said “thanks.” class ended and i walked out feeling as if i was on a different plane of the universe compared to others. that “different plane” soon changed when i forgot something in my locker and had to run to get it.

2. finishing a project in record time

uh, so, when my social studies or history class or whatever it’s called started, we went over to the library to work on a project we have due in like 2 weeks. i started from 0 here but somehow just sped through the entire project in like 35 minutes. it should have taken like 12 hours, but i somehow managed to do that. freaky.

anyway, i ask my teacher a question cause there is no way i was doing everything correctly in those 35 minutes, but to my surprise, i did. i kind of stood there just laughing, confused, and frozen all at the same time.

then class ended and i got a sandwich :3

3. the kahoot clutch

oml, this one was legendary.

so, math class started. we began by playing a kahoot. the teacher had specified that top 5 gets extra points on the next test. knowing me, i wanted first.

anyway, i started the kahoot with an incorrect answer.

“great start!” i said to myself

the wording on the the kahoot was so confusing, it was just like that extra “the“ you missed at the beginning of this sentence.

anyway, i started back in last place after being embarrassed with my red “x” on my screen for everyone to see. at question 10/25, i was in 13th.

“i suck.” i said to myself

although, we had 2 mins for each question so i was just drawing in the meantime.

around question 21/25, i was in 10th place. i was already conceding at this point. however, i thought,

”hey, what if everyone got the next one wrong except me? i’m only 1000 off from 5th.”

the genius plan. but it worked. the the next one was really confusing just like that extra “the” you missed again. hehe. anyway, it got to question 24 and i was still in 10th. i knew i wasn’t gonna win, but i was still having fun drawing so i didn’t care.

*sigh*

the last question. i decided to guess. i really didn’t care at this point. even if i guessed, i wasn’t gonna make top 5.

oh how i was wrong.

that 25% chance guess ended in my favor granting me those 1k points i needed to get to 5th. only a few people including me had gotten that one right. when that question concluded and i saw where my placing was, i was so shocked. from a joke to reality, i managed to get that +1 extra credit point.

rip that 5th place person though. i completely robbed them of those credits.

4. a surprise

so, i was walking on a path to the bus i take home until these 3 guys walk out of a door to my right. they walk behind me for like 5 seconds until one of them thought it would be “edgy” and “funny” to poke me with a lacrosse stick. i shouldn’t have to specify where they poked me since i can assume you can read between the lines.

not to mention: when people say “oh, you should have done that” “oh you should have done this.” that means nothing. i bet you would be as lost as i was despite you training years for that moment. unfortunately, humanity sucks.

anyhow, that moment felt like hours. i was so confused. i felt like when i finished that project in 35 minutes. just utter shock and confusion. i started to speedwalk after that towards a bench where a friend of mine greets me. they inform me on how they almost got suspended and i just lost it at that point. my mind has left my body.

so i sat down with some pringles and came to the only place where i can feel comfortable. the hypixel forums.



so, if you ever feel lost, the hypixel forums is always there! thanks for reading, cheers
