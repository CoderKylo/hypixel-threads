Another difference with hypixel and the difference with your example is that you have to serve 20 years and then you are free, hypixel's perma bans are permanent, that means you can't go back. It's an unfortunate situation but there are many loopholes in the system itself that can be taken advantage too, so those perma banned people can still play on hypixel, just they might lose that veteran status they had and their stats.
