Yeah that's right another rant from that car nerd on the forums. I know some of you are going to be like OMG YUKON U SUCK SQUID GAME NO OVERRATED IT BEZT SHOW!!!!!!!!!!!!! Yeah no, it's not.

Reason 1. Using pretty much the same idea as a bunch of other works.
The idea of the "Squid Game" or atleast a very very similar ideas have been used in a bunch of books, other TV shows, and other works. It should've never earned so much fame and so much hype from an idea which has been used hundreds and hundreds of times.

Reason 2. From what I know, it is repetitive.
From what I know, it's very repetitive with the games later in the show, I'm pretty sure there is a set number of games and they just repeat over and over until there is a winner.

Reason 3. The game is predictable
It gets quite predictable after a bit of watching the show, taking all the fun out of watching it.

Reason 4. The games are based purely on luck.
They are all based on luck, all intellectual actions and factors got completely ignored, therefore making the show less thrilling to watch.

Reason 5. It's the same as any other survival series
I don't have to go into much depth on this one, it's simply pretty much the same as any survival series, for example The Hunger Games.

Reason 6. The ending is very very similar to a classic series.
The "shocking" twist at the end of the Squid Game series is very very similar to the classic SAW.

Tl:dr Uses the same idea as a bunch of other works, it's repetitive, the game is predictable, the games are based on luck, it's the same as any other survival series, and the twist at the end of the series is very similar to the classic SAW.

Thanks for coming to my rant.
Anything I should fix?
Do you agree or disagree with me? Leave a like if you agree with me! If you disagree, let me know why in the comments.
