So that was a lot faster than I expected. Thought it would take another month, but nope! Earlier today I hit 300 followers! Unfortunately I would ping the person, but lately it hasn't been giving me a alert for every time someone follows me. That might be because I been getting a ton of follows daily recently. When I made my 250 follower thread back in august, I got 3-5 followers for 4 days straight, of course it calmed down after that, but that might be the reason why I don't get a alert for every follower I get, so because of that me not getting the alert, I don't actually know who my 300th follower was. I could guess, but I also feel like it would be rude if it's if it was someone else so I won't bother doing pings.

I'll probably be doing this for every follower milestone for now on, or maybe this is the last one, but just like in my last thread, I'll be putting the dates on when I hit certain followers and the threads I made for them, as well as how many days it took to get there.

September 21st 2020, 150 follower thread: https://hypixel.net/threads/150-followers.3362672/

June 9th 2021, 200 follower thread: https://hypixel.net/threads/200-followers.4311765/

August 16th 2021, 250 follower thread: https://hypixel.net/threads/250-followers.4478480/

From 150 followers to 200 followers, it took 261 days to get 50 followers. From 200 followers to 250 followers, it took 68 days to get 50 followers. This time however, from 250 followers to 300 followers, it took me 31 days to get 50 followers! It's getting faster and faster every time! However it will likely get slower from here. But I want to thank those that decided to follow me, it means a lot! Like I mentioned in my last thread, I will no longer be making follower threads for every 50 I get, for now on it will be 100. Which means the next follower milestone thread will be 400 followers! Let's see when I'll get that.

I would say this might be my favorite follower milestone thread yet, as it reminds me the good old days back when joshua was active as hell in 2017 and I remember I would stalk his profile and I would always see the big 300 number. Maybe it wasn't in 2017, but there was definitely a time where I would check his profile which had 300 followers. And now it feels weird that I'm at this number, back then I saw joshua as this giant beast who was being active everywhere and also had a long streak where there would be at least one profile comment from someone. I believe it lasted 2 months, but I'm not sure, but it was a wonderful time.

See y'all at 400 followers!
