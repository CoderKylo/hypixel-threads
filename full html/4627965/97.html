Are you one of those that are undecided of what shader to choose for your gameplay?
This list will showcase a bunch of shaders that work perfectly fine on older versions such as 1.8.9.

1) Sora Shaders

Sora is an edit of ProjectLuma, which has a pretty good performance, and it's easy on eyes.
It has some nice light shafts, and doesn't affect the vanilla style of minecraft too much.

Custom Sky Support: 7.5/10
Quality: 7.5/10
Performance: 8/10

Theme: Vanilla




2) RedHat

This shader got popular recently on curseforge, and i must say, it's one of the best fantasy stylized shaders out there.
While other shaders handle the resource pack's sky badly, this one actually blends the shader's sky with the custom one, which looks awesome and breath taking.
We can't forget the awesome lighting, fog and sky.

Custom Sky Support: 10/10
Quality: 8.5/10
Performance: 7/10

Theme: Cartoonish/Fantasy





3) UShader

UShader is a shader made by RRe36, which looks awesome.
Probably the most realistic shader in this list, it features awesome clouds, built-in motionblur, extremely detailed sun and many other things.

Custom Sky Support: None
Quality: 9/10
Performance: 7.5/10

Theme: Realism
