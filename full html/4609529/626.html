Imo apple crisp is a heavily underrated desert since it's pretty easy to make and tastes really good. It's basically apple pie with the crust but better.

Here's how to bake apple crisp. (Note that in the pictures, I'm making 3x the regular portion since I'm also making the apple crisp for friends and family. The recipe is following the recipe for one portion)

Step 1: Get all the ingredients you need.

That would be

1. Apples (preferably baking apples such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp. I'm using Macintosh apples which work fine as well)
2. Brown sugar
3. Cinnamon
4. Nutmeg
5. White flour
6. Unsalted butter or margarine
7. Quick cooking oats. Make sure they're the quick cooking ones. Most other ones are used for oatmeal and such.
8. An oven
9. A fork

Step 2: Prepare apples

Wash your apples. Skin and peel off the skin cause you don't want going into your desert. Cut them into half-inched slices and put them in a bowl. You'll need around 3-4 cups of apples.






Peeling and slicing these apples took forever

Step 3: Make the granola part of the apple crisp.

Get a big bowl.

Mix in half a cup of flour, third to half cup (depending on how sweet you want it to be) of brown sugar, a teaspoon of nutmeg and a teaspoon of cinnamon, half-one cup of quick cooking oats (depending on how much of the granola part you want), and 5-6 tablespoons of unsalted butter or margarine (butter works better for me).

Then you just mix everything really thoroughly. Make sure to try and break the clumps of butter and brown sugar as much as possible so they spread out evenly when you bake the apple crisp. Beat the mixture really hard just like how you beat your
Before I beat it up

After I beat it up

Step 4: Get ready to put everything in the oven.

Get a 6+ diameter ish baking bowl or wtv they're called, and grease them with cooking spray so that the food doesn't stick when you try to eat.

Mix in the sliced apples and generously sprinkle the granola mixture over everything. Be sure that the apples are all covered in the mixture.

Bowl's I used (three since I'm making three portions)

Apples in the bowls

After putting in the apples and granola mixture

Step 5: Bake the apple crisp yay

If you're allergic to epic apple aromas, then I would have to tell you to leave the house while these are baking.

It literally fills your whole house with an awesome apple granola smell. Anyways.

Preheat your oven to 190 celsius idfk what that is in Fahrenheit imagine being American. Set the time to 35 minutes.

Then you just wait for it to preheat and put the food in

No pictures for this one.

Step 6: Eat apple crisp

Enjoy apple crisp. Make sure to eat it with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream cause that enhances the flavours by a lot.
