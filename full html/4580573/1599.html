Durian - A food review
Made by Efic​
I was bored today so I'm blessing you guys with an effort fruit review so appreciate it <3

Introduction:
So I was bored, looking around Youtube for videos to watch. then my eyes stumbled upon a video's titled this.

Interesting right? So as an instinct, I watched the whole thing. The whole video was them trying durians out and puking for 1/3rd of the film length. And this also isn't the ONLY one. So today I'm going to make an effort fruit review about durians. Are they really that bad? Or is it just a hoax made up by Westerns?

Why you can trust me:
I am an Asian myself and I'm also confident about this since I've eaten many before and I live in a place that's rich in durians supplies. (wont tell you where though)

The Review:

Durian - Wikipedia

en.wikipedia.org

Durian Fruit: Smelly but Incredibly Nutritious

Durian fruit is a tropical fruit known for its unusual, spiky appearance and strong smell. Durian is also highly nutritious and has several health benefits.
www.healthline.com

It's very creamy when you take a first bite into it, then the sweetness and a bit of bitterness will flow straight through your tongue. This fruit is perfect for making ice cream!


Conclusion:
This fruit is heavily underrated. There were no slight hint of any taste of onion, garlic or whatever **** the people that tried it say. It doesn't even have that bad of a smell! But after all, it's all up to personal tastes and opinions. I suggest trying it out if you haven't, head to the local market grab some fresh, golden ones and give me yall opinions below.

PA: should i make a series out of this?
Have a good weekend and peace out nerds
- Efic​
