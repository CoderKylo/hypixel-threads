Hello,

I know this may sound weird and want to create confusion, but I wanted to express myself

Although Luna has an FPS boost above Badlion, they say it's great for PvP and other things.

Hey, I get a foot behind with him because of:
- Player Support Administration (I've seen not-so-good stories)
- Some Great Servers banned it, because it was having a problem with the server's AC and after they analyzed they saw the advantage over those who didn't use it (I saw that the Lunar team solved this and some remove the ban, but it doesn't erase the history )
- Unlike Badlion (due to API), lunar devs have to manually disable the mod on specific server (like freelook) when the mod is banned

And in Badlion, I didn't see these problems happen
Not to mention that badlion API gives control to the Mods and CPS Server (yes the server can configure this, even inviting the server's automatic system to ban by autoclick) that a player can have
Also, I feel that I can customize more than the Lunar
And not to mention that the badlion has mods that lunar that is not just PvP (I'm not much of spade PvP...)

So that's what I meant
By the way, as my English is not very good, I apologize for anything
