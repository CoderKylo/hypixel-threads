i make art and upload it to the hypixel forums. the art i make is usually always anime or manga style. i will now start taking requests for anime art pieces you want me to make. just send the reference and follow the rules below.

as i improve further, like i did when i stopped using tutorials and moved to replicating, i’ll try to start freehanding. i would have tried that from the start, but i just knew it wasn’t a good point to start at. using tutorials helped me learn common things with pencils when drawing and some technique.

when i moved off of yt tutorials and went to google to start replicating already existing images, i used the stuff i learned from tutorials to help shape the replication to not look like a 2nd grader made it. the killua and ikalgo one is a good example. i used basic shapes to outline the body of killua and ikalgo and then later shaped it into the real thing slowly.

and then i thought it would help if i were to dramatically step it up a notch and try to replicate an insane piece of yashiro and hanako. i incorporated some of the stuff i’ve learned from tutorials plus using light strokes on a pencil rather than hard strokes to make it easier to erase once you’ve outlined it.

a mistake i made when doing hard strokes was when i drew toga and kayo.

and as i get better, i’ll try to start freehanding like i said above, but i don’t think i’m ready for that yet so i’ll stick to manga panels and stuff.

Dunno what's the point in doing commissions if you're just gonna redo other people's art but worse but what do I know.
