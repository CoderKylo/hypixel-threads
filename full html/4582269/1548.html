I wanna make this clear, I have nothing against the actual client itself and more against the people who use it, I will explain more in the article

reasons the lunar community is bad:

1. cult like following for lunar client: i think this is self explanatory, the majority of lunar client users basically beg you to download lunar and say it basically solves every problem in the world. they love lunar so much that they basically hate on everyone for using a different client like labymod and forge

2. usually an 8-12 year old kid: self explanatory, lunar client is easy to download unlike mods like forge so you see alot of 9 and 10 year olds using it who are usually toxic and annoying, again theres nothing wrong with the client itself with this either, its just if you make your client easy to download, it will get downloaded by alot of younger kids and tbh I find them annoying, and when I was 8 I was annoying too xD.

3. usually closed minded and hates any new version even if you remove the combat system: I opened a server, and installed a plugin which replicates 1.8 pvp in 1.16 so well, it even had the same regen, fire damage and knockback as 1.8 and every kid I met who used lunar client still found something "wrong" with my server. one said that the server was extremely laggy and "unplayable", the tps was 20. also one said they wouldnt play on the server because "new textures", then I said "download a texture pack or smth that makes it look more 1.8, want me to send u one" and then claimed I was trying to give him a virus

4. "stop bowspamming": I shot a bow once at this one kid (who was 8) and he claimed I was bowspamming......

5. buys every cosmetic in the store: I met kids who spent up to 200 dollars on the lunar store, WHY. there just cosmetics

anyways thats my rant
