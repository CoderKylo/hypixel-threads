I think I know what you're talking about. It's not copy/paste (although copy/paste is indeed possible in vanilla Minecraft with the conventional keyboard shortcuts). If you open the chat window (T by default), you can use the up and down arrows to scroll through your chat history (as in things you typed -- not to be confused with messages received, which can be scrolled through via the scroll wheel). I believe your chat history stores the last 100 entries that you typed in. Depending on the version of Minecraft, that history may or may not get deleted upon leaving a world/server.
