before you dislike this thread, i'll admit that lunar has some good features and the quality of the features are pretty good, this is why i'll start with what i like about lunar client

Things I like about Lunar Client

  • The overall feeling of the client, it feels like a premium pvp client.
  • The mods, the quality of the mods, and the customizability of the mods
  • The client is organized, built with separate sections for the settings
  • Adjustable ram allocation (which doesn't work and i'll explain how)
  • Skyblock mods
Now, you're probably wondering, if the client is super good why do you not like it?
i won't be talking about how awful the community is because i'll only be judging the client itself, not the 9 year olds that come with it.

Things I hate/not like about Lunar Client

  • The ram slider. Lunar usually takes up more ram than you set it to (like 1.5 gb more depending on how bad)
  • The block-hit animation. Absolutely dreadful, the animation is smooth but not pleasing to look at.
  • Lunar eats up your resources. If you don't have 16+ gb of ram, dont download lunar.
Now, you're wondering, if there's more good than bad, then why do you not like it. Well, I see these issues pretty big and makes the client practically unusable if you have a lot of stuff in the backround, (like me)

Conclusion
Lunar Client is one of the best clients IF you have an above above average pc. Otherwise, Lunar just slows down your pc. Instead, use Forge or LabyMod
this was intentional
