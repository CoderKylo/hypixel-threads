before you blast me let me explain why survival servers should not use land claim plugins and allow griefing, stealing and raiding.

1. land claim plugins are annoying
people can use the land claim plugins to claim large areas and basically stop people from mining in a certain area which is dumb, annoying and non vanilla, also these plugins are usually scuffed and I hate them
2. disallowing griefing, stealing and adding land claim plugins is un vanilla
the fact is that allowing people to claim large areas of land isnt vanilla and its dumb, it defeats the point of survival in my opinion. I play survival to grind for hours, gear up and fight with players but when you add land claim plugins all these players have to do to retreat is hide in there house.
3. "what if the younger kids get angry about there house being destroyed"
if these younger kids arent mature enough to use common sense and hide there house and they get upset about getting stuff blown up, maybe they shouldnt play multiplayer
4. griefing is fun
griefing simply is fun and enjoyable, everyone likes blowing stuff up with TNT and withers and the fact is that land claims make survival servers unfun because theres nothing to look forward to. on griefing allowed servers I will grind for hours just to get some TNT and im always very excited to set off on an adventure an destroy some stuff. Also this adds a challenge, because when griefing is enabled you must actually hide your base and be more careful about who knows your base coords
5. griefing is a gameplay mechanics
why do you think TNT, withers, lava and flint of steel were added. the only use TNT had was to blow stuff up, it was horrible as a weapon and also not good for mining with. the fact is griefing and destruction has been a gameplay mechanic since the game started over 10 years ago so dont disable it

thanks for listening to my rant
